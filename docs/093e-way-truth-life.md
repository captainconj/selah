*Experiment 093e. Level 2 Thummim — combined word parsing.*

## The Finding

The letters of **דרך** (way) + **אמת** (truth) + **חיים** (life) also spell:

**את דרך חי מים** — "aleph-tav, way of living water."

- דרך + אמת + חיים = ד,ר,ך,א,מ,ת,ח,י,י,ם
- את + דרך + חי + מים = א,ת,ד,ר,ך,ח,י,מ,י,ם

Same letters. 54 total phrase parsings. This one stands apart.

**GV = 733, which is prime.** Indivisible.

## John 14:6

> Jesus said to him, "I am **the way**, and **the truth**, and **the life**. No one comes to the Father except through me."

The three words Jesus chose to describe himself — דרך, אמת, חיים — contain within their letters a fourth reading: **את דרך חי מים** — the aleph-tav (first and last), the way of living water.

---

## The Components

**את** (aleph-tav) — the first and last letters of the Hebrew alphabet. In the Torah, את is the untranslatable direct object marker that points at what the sentence is about. "In the beginning God created **את** the heavens and **את** the earth." Revelation 22:13: "I am the Alpha and the Omega, the first and the last."

**דרך** (way) — the same word as in John 14:6.

**חי מים** (living water) — John 4:10: "If you knew the gift of God... he would have given you **living water**." John 7:38: "Rivers of **living water** will flow from within him." Jeremiah 2:13: "They have forsaken me, the fountain of **living water**." Zechariah 14:8: "On that day **living waters** shall flow out from Jerusalem."

The way, the truth, and the life rearrange to say: the first-and-last on the way of living water.

---

## The Prime

733 is prime. The way-truth-life cannot be factored into smaller components. It is irreducible — a unity that admits no decomposition.

733 is the 130th prime. 130 = סלם (ladder) = סיני (Sinai) = 10 × 13 (completeness of love).
