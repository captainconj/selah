### Male + Female = Anointing

Reading the foundation (c=7) against the taxonomy (c=12) produces a packaging manifest:

| Foundation (c=7) | Taxonomy (c=12) | Reading |

| you shall make | the beast | fn → what |

The verb (**וכפרת**) is the function — it does the covering.

Same root, two forms. **fn** and **data**.

**מבית ומחוץ** — from inside and from outside. The function wraps the data on both faces with the same material. A lipid bilayer. A cell membrane.

Each **קן** (room/nest) is a cell. The guard proofreads. The pairs board after their kind.
