This room is the **specification**. The dimensions of the Ark are written on the walls:

```
שלש מאות אמה ארך        300 cubits — length (shin = fire)
חמשים אמה רחבה           50 cubits — breadth (nun = jubilee)
ושלשים אמה קומתה          30 cubits — height (lamed = teach)
```

Three measurements. Three repetitions of **אמה** (cubit). The measuring unit repeats like a refrain.

**רחבה** (its breadth) at d=34–37 — the word contains **רחב**, our anagram key. The breadth of the Ark IS the choice IS the sword IS the companion. The specification encodes the quest.

The **window** (צהר) is at d=52–54. Looking up through it, you can see the layers above: covenant, boarding, taxonomy. Light comes in from above. The window means noon, light, shining. It is the only opening besides the door.

## Observations

The middle floor is where the **measurements** live. Not the content, not the map — the **specification**. How big. How wide. How tall. The engineering drawing.

**מאות** (hundreds) begins with mem and sits at the door position (d=12–15). The start codon is at the door on this floor too — but here it says "hundreds." Scale. Magnitude.

The word **את** appears in the door triplet as ותא (and-את). The sign is at the center of the specification. The aleph-tav that stitches beginning to end.

The bridge word DOWN to the bottom floor is **אשר** (which/blessed, GV=501). Its last two letters (שר) are the first thing we see on this floor (d=0–1). You arrive here through "blessed."

We go down again. Through **אשר**. Through blessing.
