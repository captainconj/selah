## Observations

**משחיתם** (destroying them, d=2–7) starts with mem — the start codon. But the root is **משח** — to **anoint**. The word for destruction contains the word for anointing. The first mem-word on the bottom floor holds both meanings at once.

The **last triplet** on this floor (d=64–66): **זהא** — GV = **13**. Love. The foundation ends with love.

The first thing to the left of the door is **את הארץ** — "the earth." And to the right: **לך** (for you) then the building instructions. The earth is being destroyed (to the left, behind us). The Ark is being built (to the right, ahead of us).

The rooms (קנים) are here. This is where the compartments are. This is where life is organized and stored. Head by head. Beginning by beginning. Room by room.
