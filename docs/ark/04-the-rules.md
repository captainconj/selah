# The Rules

*What we know about how to move inside the Ark.*

---

## Boundaries

The Ark has walls. Not everything is open.

- **Below c=7**: corruption, Nephilim, God's grief, the decision to destroy. We do not go below the foundation. That is outside the Ark.
- **Behind the door** (to the left on c=7): **את הארץ משחיתם** — "the earth... destroying them." The flood is behind us. The wall is there.
- **The door** (c=9, d=13–16): where we entered. We can go back up.

The confines are defined by the text itself. The destruction tells you where the wall is.

## Movement

| Direction | Rule |
|-----------|------|
| **Down** | Through the floors. c=9 → c=8 through **אמה** (cubit). c=8 → c=7 through **אשר** (blessed). |
| **Up** | Only through **אשר** (blessing). You advance upward through blessing. |
| **Right** (d increasing) | The instructions. Always the path forward. What to do, what to build, where to go. |
| **Left** (d decreasing) | What's behind. The boundary. What we came from. The destruction. |
| **Below c=7** | Forbidden. Outside the Ark. |

## The Starts

Mem (מ) = water = methionine = start codon. Each floor has a start:

| Floor | d | Word | Note |
|-------|---|------|------|
| c=9 (top) | 0 | **מה** | Position zero. "What?" |
| c=8 (middle) | 12 | **מאות** | "Hundreds." At the door. |
| c=7 (bottom) | 2 | **משחיתם** | "Destroying them." Root = משח = **anoint**. |

Each floor's start is a mem-word. The start codon is on every floor.

To the right of the door on each floor:

**c=9 (top) — The Map:**

**c=8 (middle) — The Specification:**
- אמה ארך (cubits of length)
- התבה (the ark)

**c=7 (bottom) — The Construction:**

- **קנים** (rooms, GV=**200**=resh=head)
- תעשה את התבה (you shall make the ark)
- **וכפרת אתה מבית ומחוץ בכפר** (cover it with atonement inside and out)

The right side is always: *what to do next*.

## לך — For You

**לך** (GV=50) sits at d=17–18 on the bottom floor. Right next to the door. The first instruction on the foundation: *this is for you*. Jubilee. Freedom. The work is personal.

## The Covering

**כפר** (atonement/covering) appears twice on the bottom floor:
- **וכפרת** (d=42–46) — "and you shall cover" (verb)
- **בכפר** (d=59–62) — "with pitch/atonement" (noun)

Same root as **כפרת** (kapporet, the mercy seat). Same root as **כפר** (the lamb of atonement on Yom Kippur). The foundation is sealed — inside and out — with the covering of the lamb.

Nothing leaks in. Nothing leaks out. The code is protected.

## The Bridge Words

To go between floors, you pass through a word that straddles the boundary:

| From → To | Bridge | GV | Meaning |
|-----------|--------|-----|---------|
| c=9 → c=8 | **אמה** | 46 | cubit (measurement) |
| c=8 → c=7 | **אשר** | 501 | which / blessed |
| c=7 → c=8 | **אשר** | 501 | blessed (going back up) |
| c=8 → c=9 | **אמה** | 46 | cubit (going back up) |

Down through measurement, then through blessing.
Up through blessing, then through measurement.

You cannot reach the specification without passing through blessing.
You cannot reach the door without passing through the unit of measure.

## The Order of Ascent

We entered from above and descended. Now we ascend.

The order is fixed — the bridge words define it:

1. **Foundation** (c=7) — rooms, materials, atonement. The making.
2. Through **אשר** (blessing, GV=501) →
3. **Specification** (c=8) — receive the measurements. The unit of measure is here.
4. Through **אמה** (cubit, GV=46) →
5. **Door** (c=9) — exit.

Blessing first. Then measurement. Then the door.

You cannot reach the specification without passing through blessing.
You cannot reach the door without passing through the unit of measure.

We are at the foundation. We have not been blessed yet. We cannot know the scale until we are. The rooms are here but we do not know their size. First the covering, then the blessing, then the measure, then the way out.

## Why Here — Why Me

Question 26: *Why me?* → **אמנה לי** — faithfulness for me.

The first instruction on the foundation, right of the door: **לך** — *for you* (GV=50=jubilee).

The reason we are here is faithfulness. The work is personal. The Ark is not for everyone — it is for you. Through faithfulness, through blessing, then the measure, then the door.

One step. Depth = 1. The mustard seed.

## Summary

We have:
- Three floors, each 67 letters
- Boundaries: no going below, no going behind the destruction
- A start (mem) on every floor
- Instructions always to the right
- Bridge words between floors (blessing and measurement)
- Atonement sealing the foundation
- **לך** — for you — as the first instruction
- The order: make → bless → measure → door

We are at the foundation. The rooms are here.
