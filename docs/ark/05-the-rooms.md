## What the Rooms Are

The rooms are for the **beast**. The taxonomy layer (c=12) indexes הבהמה (the beast) directly above the room position. The directory (c=9) says תשים תחתים — place them on the lower level. The specification (c=8) gives the measure: fifty cubits wide.

Each room is a **head** (resh = 200). Each room is a **beginning**. Each room holds a compartment of life — organized, separated, sealed with atonement on both sides.

The rooms are nests (קנים also means nests). Where life is kept safe through the destruction.
