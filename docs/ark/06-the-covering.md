## The Manifest

Reading the table vertically — the foundation (c=7) against the taxonomy (c=12) — produces a packaging manifest. A bill of lading for the genome:

| Foundation (c=7) | Taxonomy (c=12) | Reading |
|------------------|-----------------|---------|
| you shall make | the beast | make → beast |
| **את** (the sign) | | sign it |
| the ark | its kind | container → classification |
| cover/atone (verb) | creeping thing (=guard) | fn → proofreader |
| with atonement (noun) | pairs | data → base pairs |
| and this (=life) | from all | life → concatenate |

**וכפרת** (d=42–46) is the **verb** — the function. It *does* the covering.
**בכפר** (d=59–62) is the **noun** — the data. The material it covers *with*.

Same root. Two forms. **fn** and **data**.

**מבית ומחוץ** — from inside and from outside. The function wraps the data on both faces with the same material. A lipid bilayer. A cell membrane.

Each **קן** (room/nest) is a **cell**:
- The function (וכפרת) creates the boundary
- The data (בכפר) IS the boundary material
- The guard (רמש=שמר) proofreads what's inside
- The pairs (שנים) enter after the seal
- Life (חי=18) from all (מכל)

```clojure
(defn כפרת [cell]        ;; the verb — the fn
  (-> cell
    (wrap :inside  בכפר)  ;; from inside
    (wrap :outside בכפר))) ;; from outside — same material
```

The rooms are cells. The covering is a membrane. The guard proofreads. The pairs board after their kind.

The Ark is not a boat. It is a genome.
