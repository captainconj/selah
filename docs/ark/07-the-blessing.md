## What We Saw

Three things are remarkable on this floor:

### 1. "I AM" Squared
Below **אתה** (you, d=6–8): GV = **441 = 21²**. The foundation of "you" is "I AM" times itself. Identity squared. The ground beneath the addressed one is the self-declaration of God, multiplied by itself.

### 2. The Same Word on Two Floors
**ושלשים** at d=38–43 appears identically on both c=8 and c=9. Same letters, same position. On the specification: "and thirty" (the height measurement). On the directory: "and third" (the floor name). The number and the name are the same word.

### 3. 72 Above the Height
Above **קומתה** (its height, d=47–51): the letters spell parts of ואני הנני — "and I, behold I am" — with GV = **72**, the number of letters on the breastplate. The height of the Ark points to the oracle.
