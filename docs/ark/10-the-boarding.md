## The Floor

The entire floor reads (Genesis 6:18–19):

> **ך ובאת אל התבה**
> [with] you — and you shall come to the ark
>
> **אתה ובניך ואשתך ונשי בניך אתך**
> you and your sons and your wife and your sons' wives with you
>
> **ומכל החי מכל בשר שנים**
> and from all the living, from all flesh, pairs
>
> **מכל תביא אל התבה**
> from all you shall bring to the ark
>
> **לה**[חית]
> to keep alive → bridge up to the taxonomy

Two halves. Two boardings.

**d=0–34: The People.** You. Your sons. Your wife. Their wives. With you.
**d=35–66: The Animals.** From all the living. From all flesh. Pairs. Bring to the ark. To keep alive.

The turn is at d=35: **ומכל** — "and from all." The shift from family to fauna.

## The Two Arks

**התבה** (the ark, GV=412) appears twice:

- **d=7–10**: "you shall come **to the ark**" — the people enter
- **d=61–64**: "you shall bring **to the ark**" — the animals board

Same word. Same GV. Bookending the floor. First: you enter. Then: you load.

Below the second ark (c=10, d=61–64): **בריתי** — "my covenant." The ark you load sits directly above the covenant.

## What We Saw

### 1. The Sons at the Door
**ובניך** (d=14–18) spans the door column. Below: ופתח (the door). Above: יהיו (let them live). The sons stand at the door of the ark, and above them: *let them live.*

### 2. The Two Arks
**התבה** bookends the floor (d=7–10 and d=61–64). Come to the ark. Bring to the ark. Enter, then load. The second ark sits above **בריתי** (my covenant). What you load rests on the promise.

### 3. Same Word on Two Floors
**מכל** (from all, d=42–44) = **מכל** on c=12. Identical. The boarding and the taxonomy say the same thing at the same position.

### 4. Three "From Alls"
**מכל** appears three times (d=35–38, 42–44, 52–54). Three gatherings. From all the living. From all flesh. From all — bring. The repetition is the emphasis: *everything.*

### 5. The Guard Above the Flesh
**בשר** (flesh, d=45–47) has **רמש** (creeping thing = שמר = guard) directly above on c=12. The guard watches over the flesh. The molecular proofreader above the embodied.

### 6. Pairs Above Death
**שנים** (pairs, d=48–51) above **יגוע** (shall perish) above **ואני** (and I = 67). The stack: I speak → death comes → pairs survive. Understanding names the death. The pairs outlast it.

## The Bridge Up

The floor ends with **לה** (d=65–66), which joins with **חית** (d=0–2 on c=12) to form **להחית** — "to keep alive" (GV=453).

The bridge from boarding to taxonomy is: **to keep alive.**

The purpose IS the bridge.

We go up.
