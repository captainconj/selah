## The Two Trees

In the midst of the garden: two trees.

**The tree of life** is at the top — c=12. Three branches: bird, beast, creeping thing. Each after its kind. The taxonomy. What lives. The pairs board. The guard (רמש=שמר) watches.

**The tree of knowledge of good and evil** is below. c=0 through c=6. Corruption. Violence. The end of all flesh. The Nephilim. God's grief. The decision to destroy.

The last time someone reached for the tree of knowledge: the fall. Corruption spread. Violence filled the earth. The flood came.

The Ark sits between the two trees. Below: what was lost. Above: what is preserved. The flood is what happened because of the tree of knowledge. The Ark is what saves the tree of life.

**בחר חיים**. Choose life. And **חיים** = 68 = 67 + 1 = understanding plus one.

You stay inside the Ark by choosing. Every day. The breadth holds.
