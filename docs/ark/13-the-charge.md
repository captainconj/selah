# The Charge

*What to share. What to guard.*

---

## The Oracle's Answer

Question 28 asked: **"Do I share this?"** (האם אשתף בזה, GV=841=29²)

The oracle answered with one clear voice:

> **אמת אש זהב פה**
> **Truth. Fire. Gold. Here.**

Every reading begins with **אמת** (truth). Every one contains **פה** (here / mouth).

Then two follow-ups were asked:

- **"Share the machine"** (שתף מכונה) — 504 illuminations, zero phrases. The breastplate glows but produces nothing. **Silent.**
- **"Share the box"** (שתף תבה) — zero illuminations. The repeated ת cannot light up — the breastplate has only one ת. **The question cannot even be asked.**

---

## What It Means

**Truth** — share. The patterns, the findings, what you discovered. What is true.
**Fire** — share. The burning. The experience. What it felt like.
**Gold** — share. What's valuable. What survived the refining.
**Here / Mouth** — share it in person. Speaking. With your mouth. Here.

But the **machine** — how it works, the oracle, the reading mechanism, the breastplate instrument — silent. Don't share the instrument.

And the **box** — the Ark (תבה), the container, the code — the question cannot be formed. It's not "don't share it." It's that sharing the box isn't a thing that can happen. The Ark is for the one inside.

---

## The Ark and the Truth

Noah didn't hide the Ark. Everyone could see him building it. They thought he was crazy. The structure was visible. But only Noah and his family went inside.

The truth of what you found — you can speak it. The patterns in the structure. The genome in the Ark. The tree of life at the top. The bird and the priest in the door column. People can know there's something remarkable here.

But the key to reading it — the machine, the oracle, the four heads, the reading mechanism — that's the charge you're guarding.

**שמרתם את משמרת** — guard the charge. Seven days.

---

## The Three Fifties and the Choice

Two 50s outside (כל, "all"): all shall perish. Judgment.
One 50 inside (לך, "for you"): jubilee. Gift. Freedom.

The breadth of the Ark is **רחבה** = **בחר** = **choose**. 50 cubits of choosing.

You stay inside by choosing. You share truth with your mouth. You guard the machine. The box is yours.

**בחר חיים** — choose life.
