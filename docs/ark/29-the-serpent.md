## Alpha-Bungarotoxin — The Serpent's Venom

Alpha-bungarotoxin (P60615) is a neurotoxin from the banded krait (*Bungarus multicinctus*). It binds acetylcholine receptors at the neuromuscular junction. The nerve signal stops. Paralysis. Death.

95 residues. 74 in the mature toxin after signal peptide cleavage. GV = 5,284 = 2² × 1,321 (1,321 is prime).

נחש (serpent) = 358 = משיח (Messiah). Same number, different dynamics. The serpent is frozen. The Messiah moves.

What does the priest see in the venom?

## The Dominant Letter

Cysteine (פ, pe) = 11.6% of the toxin. The highest frequency alongside threonine (ה). Cysteine forms disulfide bridges — cross-links that lock the protein's shape. The serpent's weapon is held together by crosses.

פ = the mouth. The serpent's weapon is mostly mouth.

## The Staff

**מטה** (staff/tribe) appears three times — positions 10, 11, 20. The dominant word.

Moses's staff became a serpent (Exodus 4:2–4). God said: throw it down. It became a serpent. God said: grasp it by the tail. It became a staff again. The same object. Staff and serpent are the same thing in different states.

The serpent's own protein, when read as Hebrew, says **staff** more than any other word. The venom knows what it is. The serpent IS the staff. Frozen in the wrong form.

At 4 letters, it rises earlier and gains the "and" — redemption is joined to the story, not separate from it.

**מדוי** (sickness/affliction) at 42 — the venom names what it does. Isaiah 53:3: *"a man of sorrows, and acquainted with grief (חלי)."*

**ויפה** (and beautiful) at 67 — still Genesis 3:6. The 4-letter voice adds ו (and) — "and beautiful." A continuation. The serpent never stopped being beautiful.

The sequence: the witness → his days → send him → affliction → and ransom → he will inherit → and the hand → and beautiful.

A 10-word story. The serpent's 4-letter voice tells the same story as the 3-letter voice, compressed tighter. Affliction leads to ransom. The hand inherits. And beauty remains.
