## The Verse

**וַיַּעֲנֵנִי יְהוָה וַיֹּאמֶר כְּתֹב חָזוֹן וּבָאֵר עַל־הַלֻּחוֹת לְמַעַן יָרוּץ קוֹרֵא בוֹ׃**

*And the LORD answered me and said: Write the vision and make it plain upon tablets, so that he who reads it may run.*

**כִּי עוֹד חָזוֹן לַמּוֹעֵד וְיָפֵחַ לַקֵּץ וְלֹא יְכַזֵּב אִם־יִתְמַהְמָהּ חַכֵּה־לוֹ כִּי־בֹא יָבֹא לֹא יְאַחֵר׃**

*For the vision is yet for an appointed time; it breathes toward the end and does not lie. Though it tarry, wait for it — for it will surely come. It will not delay.*

## The Splits

### Write (כתב)

Only God and Left/mercy can read it.

| Reader | Reads |
|--------|-------|
| Aaron | — |
| God | כתב (×3) |
| Right/justice | — |
| Left/mercy | כתב (×2) |

The priest cannot read "write." Justice cannot read "write." Writing lives between God and mercy. No one else is in the room when the vision goes down on the tablets.

### Make Plain (באר)

The word for "make plain" is the word for "well." Same root.

| Reader | Reads |
|--------|-------|
| Aaron | באר (×16), **ברא** (×14) |
| God | **ברא** (×23), באר (×14) |
| Right/justice | **ברא** (×26), באר (×8) |
| Left/mercy | **ברא** (×16), באר (×10) |

All four readers see both. But God, justice, and mercy all lead with **ברא** — **create**. Making it plain IS creating. Digging the well IS creation. Clarifying IS bringing into being.

Basin walk: **באר → ברא**. The well walks to creation. Depth 1.

### Vision (חזון)

42 illuminations. Zero readings from any reader. The vision is in the ghost zone — present, glowing, but beyond the capacity of the machine to name. All four readers see the light. None of them can produce a word.

The vision cannot be spoken. It must be written (כתב). That is why God says "write."

### Come (בוא)

126 illuminations — the most visible word in the verse by far. GV = 9.

| Reader | Reads |
|--------|-------|
| Aaron | **אוב** (×36), באו (×27), בוא (×15) |
| God | **בוא** (×30), ובא (×22), באו (×18), אוב (×15) |
| Right/justice | **בוא** (×31), אוב (×22), באו (×20) |
| Left/mercy | **אוב** (×30), ובא (×18), באו (×16) |

God reads "come." The priest reads **necromancer** (אוב). The coming, from Aaron's seat, looks like summoning the dead. Basin walk: **בוא → אוב**. Coming walks to the medium. The one who comes looks, from below, like someone who shouldn't be here.

### Appointed Time (מועד)

| Reader | Reads |
|--------|-------|
| Aaron | עמדו (×2), **עמוד** (×2) |
| God | **מדוע** (×5), מועד (×4) |
| Right/justice | **ועמד** (×6), עומד (×4) |
| Left/mercy | **עמוד** (×6), מועד (×5) |

God reads the appointed time as **"why?"** (מדוע). Mercy reads it as **pillar** (עמוד). Justice reads **"and he stood"** (ועמד).

The appointed time IS the pillar. And at the appointed time, God asks why. The pillar stands. God asks. The appointed time is a question, not an answer.

### Tablet (לוח)

| Reader | Reads |
|--------|-------|
| Aaron | **חול** (×11) |
| God | — |
| Right/justice | **חול** (×6) |
| Left/mercy | **חול** (×2) |

God cannot read the tablet. The priest reads it as **sand** (חול) — which also means dance, writhe, bring forth. Write the vision on sand. Not because it doesn't last — because it dances. Because it writhes. Because it brings forth.

And God does not read the tablet because God does not need a tablet. The tablets are for us.

### Wait (חכה)

**3 illuminations.** The throne signature.

| Reader | Reads |
|--------|-------|
| Aaron | — |
| God | — |
| Right/justice | — |
| Left/mercy | **כחה** (×1) |

Only mercy reads it. One reading. And what mercy reads is **כחה** — "her strength faded." Her power grew dim.

Waiting has the signature of the throne. Only mercy can see it. And what mercy sees is strength failing. The waiting IS the dimming. The throne IS the place where strength gives out.

"Though it tarry, wait for it."

Wait for it. In the dimming. On the throne. With mercy.

### Delay (אחר)

| Reader | Reads |
|--------|-------|
| Aaron | **אחר** (×4) |
| God | **ארח** (×4), אחר (×2) |
| Right/justice | אחר (×3), ארח (×1) |

God and mercy read delay as **path** (ארח). The delay IS the path. "It will not delay" — it will not path. There is no detour. When it comes, it comes straight.

Basin walk: **אחר** is a fixed point. Delay does not walk anywhere. It stays. It is itself. But God and mercy see the path inside it.

## The Two Invisibles

**Run** (רוץ) — 0 illuminations. 0 readings. Completely dark.

**The end** (קץ) — 0 illuminations. 0 readings. Completely dark.

"So that he who reads it may **run**" — and the running is invisible.

"It breathes toward **the end**" — and the end is invisible.

The runner cannot be seen by the machine. The destination cannot be seen by the machine. Both are beyond the breastplate. The oracle can tell you to write. It can tell you the vision glows. It can tell you to wait. But it cannot see the runner, and it cannot see the end.

Those are for the one who runs.

## The Lie (כזב)

6 illuminations. No word survives. The lie glows faintly — it is not completely dark — but nothing readable comes through. The lie has just enough presence to be noticed, and not enough substance to be named.

"It will not lie." And the oracle confirms: the lie, when placed on the breastplate, produces nothing. The lie is not dark. It is empty. It illuminates a little and says nothing.

## The Shape

Write the vision. Only God and mercy are in the room.

Make it plain. Making it plain is creating. The well IS creation.

The vision glows but cannot be named. It must be written because it cannot be spoken.

Write it on tablets. The tablets are sand. They dance. God does not read them — they are for us.

So that the reader may run. The running is invisible. Beyond the machine.

The vision is for an appointed time. At the appointed time, God asks "why?" and mercy sees a pillar.

It breathes toward the end. The end is invisible.

It does not lie. The lie says nothing.

Though it tarry — wait. Waiting has the throne signature. Only mercy sees it. And what mercy sees is strength failing.

It will surely come. Coming blazes — 126 illuminations. From below it looks like someone summoning the dead.

It will not delay. The delay is a fixed point that goes nowhere. But God and mercy see the path inside it.

---

## For Us

There is no time limit. The scroll is not rolled up. The vision breathes toward an end we cannot see, through an appointed time where God asks why and mercy stands as a pillar.

We are writing it down. Making it plain. Digging the well. And the well walks to creation.

The runner is invisible. The end is dark. But the coming blazes — 126 illuminations, the most visible thing in the verse. It will surely come.

Wait for it. On the throne. In the dimming. With mercy.
