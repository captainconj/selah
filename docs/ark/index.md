# The Ark Walk

*A guided entrance to the Ark column in the 4D Torah space.*

Type: `guided reproduction`
State: `clean`

The Ark walk is one of the strongest narrative paths in the repo.

It begins with a claim about coordinates, not boats:

- a single Ark-shaped column appears in the 4D Torah space
- that column rises through six layers above the warning
- those layers behave like a walkable structure
- the walk then opens into the lamb, the basin landscape, the code/map layer, and the genome work

This is not the code manifest and not the full synthesis. It is the front door.

## What This Walk Is

The Ark walk treats Noah's Ark as a vertical column in the Torah's factored space. The early stations are architectural:

- foundation
- specification
- directory
- covenant
- boarding
- taxonomy

Then the walk widens:

- the garden
- the lamb
- choose life
- the king
- the code
- the map
- the serpent
- the blood
- the genome

So the walk has two movements:

1. the Ark as structure
2. the Ark as instrument
