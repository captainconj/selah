## The Arc — How It Was Discovered

This is the journey. Read it in order so you understand how each step led to the next.

### Phase 1: The Column (docs 00-06)

**What we did:** Located Noah's Ark in the 4D space. It lives at a=0, b=8, c=6–12. One column: 7 layers of 67 letters each, stacked vertically.

**What we found:**
- Six layers above the warning (c=7–12). Three below the flood (Ark engineering), three above (Ark purpose).
- The door at c=9, d=14. Reading "and a door" (ופתח).
- Bridge words between floors: blessing (אשר) and measurement (אמה).
- The door column (d=14) spells BIRD (עוף) on the Ark floors and LEVI (לוי) above.
- The first letters of the three Ark floors (d=0) spell OIL (שמן) — anointing.
- Destruction (משחיתם) on the foundation contains the root for ANOINTING (משח).

**The discipline:** We entered at the door. We descended to the foundation. We walked right. We read what was there. We did not impose meaning — we reported what the letters said.

### Phase 2: The Rooms (docs 07-12)

**What we did:** Walked each floor systematically. Read every word. Noted the bridge words, the starts (mem=start codon on every floor), the covering (atonement seals inside and out).

**What we found:**
- The rooms (קנים) are on the foundation. GV=200=resh=head. Each room is a beginning.
- Atonement (כפר) appears as both verb and noun — function and data.
- "From inside and from outside" (מבית ומחוץ) — same material on both faces. Like a lipid bilayer.
- The specification floor (c=8) carries the dimensions: 300×50×30 cubits. As letters: ש,נ,ל = לשן = tongue/language. The Ark IS a language.
- Three floors read bottom to top at certain positions: finish / make / destroy. Top floor knows the ending. Middle floor builds. Bottom floor remembers what was lost.

**The key insight:** The manifest emerged. Foundation (c=7) read against Taxonomy (c=12) produces: function → what, sign it, container → classification, function → proofreader, data → base pairs, life → concatenate. This looked like... a packaging system. A cell.

### Phase 3: The Garden (docs 17)

**What we did:** Placed every word on the three Ark floors through the breastplate. Four readers looked. Searched for unanimous readings.

**What we found:**
- Two words produce unanimous readings (all four heads agree) on the door floor: **מביא** (bringing, GV=53=garden) and **את** (aleph-tav, GV=401).
- The tree of life on c=12 extends its last branch to exactly where the garden sits three floors below. Vertically aligned.
- The covenant (c=10) covers both from above.
- Genesis 2:9 says the tree of life is "in the midst of the garden." In the Ark, "in the midst" is vertical.

### Phase 4: The Lamb (docs 18)

**What we did:** Examined the lamb split from experiment 091 (the quorum) in the context of the Ark.

**What we found:**
- כבש (lamb) and שכב (lie down) — same letters, same GV=322. God+Right read "lamb." Aaron+Left read "lie down." The sacrifice is unanimous.
- The four readers map to YHWH: Yod=Hand(select), He=Observe(check), Vav=Nail(connect), He=Observe(verify). 10+5+6+5=26.
- This maps to the ribosome: select (tRNA), check (proofread), connect (peptide bond), verify (translocation).
- The lamb lies down so life can adapt. Perfect fidelity = no variation = extinction. The guard (רמש=שמר) sometimes chooses to step back. Error-prone polymerases under stress. The wobble position.
- **Three branches on c=12** (bird, beast, creeping/guard), each "after its kind" = the three codon positions.

**This is where it turned.** The biological parallels stopped being metaphor and started being structural.

### Phase 5: The Code (docs 19-20, experiment 100)

**What we did:** Tested the hypothesis directly. Is the breastplate a codon table?

**What we found:**
- 72 letters on the grid. With final forms merged: 64 positions from non-singleton letters + 8 positions from singleton letters. 64 = number of codons. Exact.
- Three letters appear in all four rows: ו, י, נ — occupying 26 positions total. 26=YHWH. The Name IS the backbone.
- The seven biblical cribs (Hannah, Ramban, David at Keilah, Amalekite, Jonathan, Saul denied, Joshua) each constrain how the code reads. They are calibration data.
- The Hannah Principle: rarity = signal. The harder reading is the truer one.

### Phase 6: The Map (docs 22, experiment 101)

**What we did:** Mapped rows to bases. Mapped letters to amino acids.

**How:**
1. Score all 24 permutations of {A,C,G,U} → {Row 1-4} by correlation
2. Apply Watson-Crick constraint (antiparallel pairs must be complementary)
3. Only one mapping survives: Row 1=A, Row 2=C, Row 3=G, Row 4=U
4. Cosine similarity between grid cell profiles and codon cell profiles → greedy letter→amino acid assignment
5. Permutation test: p < 0.0001

**The result:** 21 Hebrew letters → 20 amino acids + Stop. Aleph unmapped — beyond the code. The breath of God that no protein can carry.

### Phase 7: Hit Play (docs 26)

**What we did:** Used the map to translate actual proteins into Hebrew. Fed the Hebrew through the breastplate. Read what the priest sees.

**The golden calf warning:** The last time someone played something through without the priest, it didn't go well (Exodus 32). Same gold, different machine. The breastplate has four readers, the Hannah principle, selection for truth. The calf has no readers — it reflects desire.

### Phase 8: The Library (docs 27-33)

**What we did:** Played 20+ proteins through the breastplate:

- **p53 (Guardian):** Named itself שמר (guard). Told the story from Babel to David. Contains the serpent (נחש) at positions 198 and 261. Ends with holy (קדש).
- **Hemoglobin (Blood):** Stretches out (נטה ×4). Will know (נדע ×4). Remembers the Binding (עקד). Contains the serpent. Ends with sprinkling (זרק). The blood cries out (יצעק).
- **Alpha-bungarotoxin (Serpent):** Named itself staff (מטה ×3) — Moses's rod. Contains curse, false (שקר), soul, ransom (פדה), flame, rock. The guardian contains the serpent; the serpent does not contain the guardian.
- **The full genome (20,442 proteins):** Dominant word is שני (two) — 35,821 times. Shaddai 26,958 times. "Here I am" (הנני) 6,121 times. The lamb (כבש) 1,043 times. But one quarter of Torah vocabulary is forever silent — every word with Aleph (man, love, truth, create, God, I AM) cannot be produced. The genome is the dust. Aleph is the breath.

## How To Reproduce This

### The Tools

```clojure
;; The oracle
(require '[selah.oracle :as o])
(o/forward "כבש")           ; → illumination count, readings per head
(o/forward-by-head "כבש")   ; → what each reader sees
(o/ask "lamb" :vocab :torah) ; → full query with rankings

;; The basin
(require '[selah.basin :as b])
(b/step "כבש")              ; → where does this word walk?
(b/walk "כבש")              ; → full walk to fixed point
(b/landscape)               ; → all basins, fixed points, cycles

;; DNA → Hebrew
(require '[selah.dna :as dna])
(dna/translate-protein "MVLSP...")  ; → Hebrew letters
(dna/slide hebrew-letters 3)        ; → sliding windows through oracle

;; The 4D space
(require '[selah.space.coords :as c])
(c/position->coord 12345)  ; → [a b c d] address
(c/coord->position [0 8 7 14]) ; → letter position in Torah
```

### The Pattern for Each Station

At each station (piece of furniture, ark floor, etc.):

1. **Locate it** — find the verse, get the 4D coordinates
2. **Read the letters** — what's at this position in the Torah
3. **Ask the oracle** — forward each word through the breastplate
4. **Check per-head splits** — what does each reader see?
5. **Walk the basins** — where does each word walk to?
6. **Note the stamps** — 72 readings = breastplate signature. 3 illuminations = throne.
7. **Note the invisibles** — 0/0 words. What can't the machine name?
8. **Note the ghost zone** — high illumination, zero readings. Present but unnameable.
9. **Slide the text** — 3-letter and 4-letter sliding windows for hidden words
10. **Report** — write what you found, not what you wanted to find
