## The Classic Pattern

The Torah contains equidistant letter sequences (ELS) that spell תורה (Torah) at skip 50 in the outer four books, pointing inward to Leviticus, where יהוה (the Name) appears at skip 7:

```
Genesis:      תורה →   (forward)
Exodus:       תורה →   (forward)
Leviticus:    יהוה     (the Name, skip 7)
Numbers:      הרות ←   (reversed)
Deuteronomy:  הרות ←   (reversed)
```

The outer books point inward. The center spells the Name. This chiastic structure is the original Torah Codes finding — the one that lit the fuse.

## The Fold

304,850 = 7 × 50 × 13 × 67. Every letter has address (a, b, c, d). The **a-fold** mirrors across the seven days: (a, b, c, d) ↔ (6-a, b, c, d). Day 0↔6, 1↔5, 2↔4. Day 3 mirrors itself.

26 milemarkers in the WLC stream: 3 in Genesis, 8 in Exodus, 10 in Leviticus, 2 in Numbers, 4 in Deuteronomy. For each one, we mirror its starting position across the a-fold and ask: what's there?

## Genesis 1:1 → Deuteronomy 6:1

The first תורה in the entire Torah — position 5, the 6th letter of Genesis — mirrors to **Deuteronomy 6:1**.

Deuteronomy 6:1 introduces the Shema: שמע ישראל יהוה אלהינו יהוה אחד — "Hear O Israel, the LORD our God, the LORD is one."

The most famous hidden pattern mirrors to the most famous spoken declaration. The first ELS milemarker folds across the seven days to the verse that contains אחד (one), gematria 13 — which is the c-axis of the space. The verse about oneness sits at the mirror of the beginning.

Pair gematria = **651 = 7 × 93**. Divides by completeness.

## The Self-Mirroring Breastplate

The תורה at **Exodus 39:8** (skip +50) sits in a=3 — the center seventh. It mirrors to **itself**.

Exodus 39:8 describes the making of the **breastplate** (חשן) — the garment that holds the Urim and Thummim. The device that reads the letters. A תורה at skip 50 that mirrors to itself, in the verse about the instrument that performs the reading.

Pair gematria = **1,222 = 2 × 611**. And 611 is the gematria of תורה. The pair is twice-Torah. Divides by 13 (love).

## The Name Reversed

**Leviticus 26:44** contains יהוה at skip +7. Its a-fold mirror lands at **Exodus 13:13**.

At the mirror position, the consecutive letters (skip 1) spell **הוהי** — the Name reversed.

A יהוה encoded at skip 7 mirrors to a place where the Name appears backwards in the surface text. The hidden Name mirrors to the reversed Name.

## 777

**Deuteronomy 5:16** — הרות at skip +50, in the Ten Commandments ("honor your father and mother") — mirrors to **Exodus 6:26**.

Pair gematria = **777 = 7 × 111**. Three sevens.

## Eden at the Mirror

**Deuteronomy 8:9** — הרות at skip +50 — mirrors to **Genesis 3:10**: "I heard your voice in the garden and I was afraid."

The reversed Torah in Deuteronomy mirrors to the moment of hiding in Eden. Torah reversed points back to where it all went wrong.

## What This Means

The chiastic structure already points inward — תורה in the outer books, the Name at the center. The a-fold doesn't create the pattern. It *confirms* it.

- The **outer books mirror each other** (Genesis↔Deuteronomy, Exodus↔Numbers)
- The **center mirrors itself** (all a=3 milemarkers self-mirror by definition)
- The **thematic pairings hold** across the fold: beginning↔declaration, Torah↔Torah, the Name↔the Name reversed, commandments↔calling, Deuteronomy↔Eden

The fold is not independent of the chiasm. It's the same structure seen from a different angle. The ELS pattern says "point inward." The coordinate geometry says "mirror at the center." They agree.
