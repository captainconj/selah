
# Son of Man = Stone of Blood

Type: `synthesis`
State: `mixed`

*Experiment 093c. Level 2 Thummim — combined word parsing.*

---

## The Finding

The letters of **בן אדם** (son of man) also spell **אבן דם** (stone of blood).

- בן (son) + אדם (man) = ב,ן,א,ד,ם
- אבן (stone) + דם (blood) = א,ב,ן,ד,ם

Same five letters. Two readings. No other dictionary parsings exist.

**GV = 97, which is prime.** Indivisible.

---

## What the Tradition Knows

**"Son of man"** (בן אדם) is the most common self-designation Jesus uses in the Gospels — over 80 times. It draws on Daniel 7:13: "one like a son of man, coming with the clouds of heaven." In Ezekiel, God addresses the prophet as "son of man" over 90 times.

**"Stone"** (אבן) is the cornerstone rejected by the builders (Psalm 118:22, quoted in Matthew 21:42, Acts 4:11, 1 Peter 2:7). Daniel 2:34 — "a stone was cut out without hands" that destroys the statue of the kingdoms.

**"Blood"** (דם) is the blood of the covenant (Exodus 24:8, Matthew 26:28). The life is in the blood (Leviticus 17:11).

The son of man is the stone. The stone bleeds. The cornerstone is the blood of the covenant. These are not separate doctrines — they are the same five letters.

---

## The Prime

97 is the 25th prime. It cannot be factored. The son of man cannot be decomposed into simpler components. He is what he is.

97 = the GV of אמן (amen, when final-nun=700) by a different reckoning. But in standard gematria, the son of man stands alone at 97. Prime. Irreducible.
