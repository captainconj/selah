# Lamb's Blood = Glory of the Name

*Experiment 093d. Level 2 Thummim — combined word parsing.*

Type: `synthesis`
State: `mixed`

---

## The Finding

The letters of **כבש** (lamb) + **דם** (blood) also spell **כבד שם** — "glory" + "name."

- כבש + דם = כ,ב,ש,ד,ם
- כבד + שם = כ,ב,ד,ש,ם

Same five letters. The lamb's blood IS the glory of the name.

**כבד (glory/heavy) has GV = 26 = יהוה.** The glory of the Name is the Name itself.

## Psalm 24

> Lift up your heads, O gates! And be lifted up, O ancient doors, that the King of glory may come in.
> Who is this King of glory? The LORD of hosts, he is the King of **glory** (כבוד).
> — Psalm 24:9-10

When the lamb's blood is parsed by the Thummim, it reads "glory of the name" — **שם מלך כבד** — "the name of the king of glory."

The answer to "who is the king of glory?" is written in the letters of the lamb and its blood.

---

## The Extension: Lamb + King + Blood

Adding **מלך** (king) to the lamb and blood yields 18 phrases. Among them:

- **שם מלך כבד** — "the name of the king of glory"
- **מלך כבד שם** — "king, glory, name"
- **מלך שכב דם** — "the king lies down in blood"

The king of glory lies down in blood. The lamb is the king. The blood is the glory. The lying down is the sacrifice.
