## The Finding

The letters of **אב** (father) + **בן** (son) + **רוח** (spirit) yield 42 phrase parsings. Among them:

- **בוא חן רב** — "great grace comes"
- **ברא חן בו** — "grace created in him"
- **באר חן בו** — "a well of grace in him"
- **בוא רב חן** — "great grace comes" (alternate order)

**GV = 269, which is prime.** Indivisible.

---

## The Letters

אב + בן + רוח = א,ב,ב,ן,ר,ו,ח

These seven letters — the Trinity in Hebrew — rearrange to say "great grace comes." Not by design. By the letters themselves.

חן (grace) = 58 = נח (Noah/rest, reversed). Grace is rest seen from the other direction.

רב (great/many) = 202 = בר (son/grain, reversed). Greatness and sonship are reflections.

בוא (come/enter) = 9 = 3². The coming is the square of three.

---

## Also Present

- **חרב בן או** — "sword, son, or" — the sword also lives in these letters
- **ברח בן או** — "flee, son, or" — and the flight

The Trinity contains grace AND the sword. Coming AND fleeing. The same letters hold both.

---

## The Prime

269 is prime. Father + son + spirit cannot be factored. The Trinity is irreducible — not three separate things multiplied together, but a single prime.

---

## The Number 42

42 phrase parsings from 7 letters. 42 = 6 × 7. The number of generations from Abraham to Christ (Matthew 1:17). The number of stations in the wilderness (Numbers 33). From the letters of the Trinity, 42 ways to read.
