## שלום = שם לו — Peace Is a Name for Him

### The Talmudic source

This is **known**. Shabbat 10b states: **שלום שמו של הקב"ה** — "Shalom is the name of the Holy One, Blessed be He." One is forbidden to say "shalom" in a bathhouse because it is a name of God.

### The prophetic source

Judges 6:24 — Gideon builds an altar and names it **יהוה שלום** — "The LORD is Peace." Peace is literally made into a name for God.

Isaiah 9:6 — "His **name** shall be called... Prince of **Peace**." The name and the peace are the same letters. שם (name) lives inside שלום (peace).

Micah 5:5 — "And this one shall be peace" (והיה זה שלום). The Messiah IS peace, not merely brings it.

### The Thummim's precision

The specific form שם לו — "a name **for him**" — implies the name is given. Philippians 2:9: "God has given him the name that is above every name." The name is bestowed. Peace is the name bestowed on him.

## חיים = חי ים — Life Is a Living Sea

### The chain of living water

- Genesis 2:10 — A river flows from Eden to water the garden
- Jeremiah 2:13 — "My people have forsaken me, the fountain of **living water** (מים חיים)"
- Jeremiah 17:13 — "The LORD, the fountain of living water"
- Ezekiel 47:1-12 — The river from the temple heals the Dead Sea; "everything will live where the river goes"
- Zechariah 14:8 — "Living waters shall flow from Jerusalem"
- John 4:10-14 — "He would have given you **living water**... a spring welling up to eternal life"
- John 7:37-38 — "Rivers of **living water** will flow from within him"
- Revelation 22:1 — "The river of the water of life, flowing from the throne of God and of the Lamb"

The standard biblical phrase is מים חיים — "living waters." The Thummim reads it reversed: חי ים — "living sea." Not a stream but an ocean. Life is not a trickle. Life is a living sea.

### The number

חיים = 68 = 67 + 1. Life is understanding (בינה = 67) plus one. One step beyond understanding. Adjacent. "Choose life" (Deuteronomy 30:19).

## עולם = עם לו — Eternity Is a People for Him

### The Hosea connection

Hosea 1:9 — God names Hosea's son **לא עמי** (Lo-Ammi, "Not My People"). The severest judgment.

Hosea 2:23 — The reversal: "I will say to Lo-Ammi, **'You are my people'** (עמי אתה)."

Romans 9:25-26 — Paul applies Hosea to the Gentiles: "I will call 'my people' those who are not my people."

1 Peter 2:10 — "Once you were not a people, but now you are the people of God."

The Thummim finds עם לו (a people for him) inside עולם (eternity). Eternity is not infinite empty time. Eternity is populated. It is having a people. The world-to-come is not an abstraction — it is relational.

### The covenant formula

Ezekiel 37:27 — "I will be their God, and they shall be **my people**."

Revelation 21:3 — "The tabernacle of God is with men... they shall be **his people**."

Titus 2:14 — Christ "gave himself for us to purify for himself **a people for his own possession**."

The phrase **עם לו** ("a people for him") is almost a technical formula in covenant theology. And the Thummim finds it inside עולם ("eternity").

## Also: אלהים = אהל ים — God Is a Tent of the Sea

Elohim decomposes into **אהל** (tent) + **ים** (sea). God is a tent pitched over the waters.

Genesis 1:2 — The Spirit of God (רוח **אלהים**) hovering over the face of the waters. The name used exclusively in the creation narrative (Genesis 1:1-2:3) describes what God does in that narrative: tent over the deep.

John 1:14 — The Word "**tabernacled** (ἐσκήνωσεν) among us." The Greek verb literally means "pitched a tent." Elohim = tent of the sea.

Isaiah 40:22 — God "stretches out the heavens like a curtain, and spreads them like a **tent** to dwell in."

Also present: **לאה ים** — "Leah's sea." God's name contains the unloved wife. Leah, whose eyes were weak, who was not chosen, who was given — her name lives inside Elohim.
