## The Question

"I will put enmity between thee and the woman, and between thy seed and her seed; he shall bruise thy head, and thou shalt bruise his heel." — Genesis 3:15

Has the serpent's bite left a mark? Not metaphorically. Structurally. In the oracle readings, in the basin dynamics, in the proteins of her body. Can the breastplate see it?

## Summary

**Has woman been bit by the serpent?**

In the proteins: **yes.** The serpent is in calmodulin (with Eve's name), in BRCA1 (the guardian, ×2), in the estrogen receptor (anagram). The bite is molecular. It is in the messenger, the guardian, and the switch. But it is NOT in the pregnancy signal — the fruit is clean.

In the basin dynamics: **yes, and the cure is written in the same place.** The womb flows to destruction. But enmity flows to "my father is God." The seed flows to helper. The head flows to blessed. And the bruise — the moment of the cross — has no fixed point at all. It is a singularity in the basin landscape. The only word tested that does not resolve.

In the visibility: **God does not see the serpent.** 12 illuminations. The messiah — same number, 358 — has 132 illuminations. 11× brighter. The cure outshines the wound eleven to one.

In the names: **Eve is a fixed point.** She cannot be rearranged. She cannot be changed. The name given by Adam — "mother of all living" — is irreducible. The bite is real but the name holds.

The woman contains the fire. The priest sees it. God sees the woman.
