# Experiment 111: Daniel's Thrones

*Walking Daniel 7 through the breastplate. Five stations. The Aramaic vision meets the Hebrew oracle.*

## The Question

Daniel 7 is the fourth throne-room vision. Four beasts rise from the sea. Thrones are set up. The Ancient of Days sits. One like a son of man comes on the clouds. Dominion is given.

The text is in Aramaic, not Hebrew. Many words share roots with Hebrew but differ in form. We test both Aramaic originals AND their Hebrew cognates through the oracle — watching what the breastplate does with a language that isn't its own, but shares its alphabet and its roots.

We know from experiments 108-110:
- Throne (כסא) = 3 illuminations across all visions. Nearly invisible.
- Eagle (נשר) = ghost zone, 60 illuminations, zero readings. Confirmed 4×.
- Man (אדם) = 9 illuminations. Nearly invisible. Confirmed across all visions.
- Earth (ארץ) = 0/0. Completely invisible. Confirmed 3×.
- God (אלהים) basin → אליהם (to them). Confirmed 3×.
- God does not read "holy." Confirmed 4×.
- King (מלך) = invisible (0/0 in Revelation).

## Method

1. Walk 5 stations through Daniel 7 by content
2. At each station: query Aramaic originals AND Hebrew cognates through `o/forward-by-head` and `basin/walk`
3. Slide 3-letter and 4-letter windows across the Aramaic text — treating Daniel's words like a protein
4. Cross-reference with experiments 103-110

Daniel 7 has 2,009 letters. Station 5 (the interpretation) is by far the longest at 918 letters.

## What Daniel Adds

### 1. The Aramaic Wheel is Invisible

Daniel uses גלגל for wheel. The breastplate returns **zero**. Not ghost zone — zero illuminations. The Aramaic wheel doesn't even glow. But the Hebrew wheel (אופן) has GV=137 and 126 illuminations. The breastplate only reads wheels in Hebrew.

### 2. Dominion → Satan

שלטן (dominion) basin→**לשטן (Satan)**. The breastplate reads the accuser inside every claim of dominion. This appears in the key word query, in the sliding windows (positions 78, 122, 389), and again at Station 4. The dominion given to the son of man: the breastplate sees the adversary in it.

### 3. Four → I Will Cross Over

ארבע (four) basin→**אעבר** (I will cross over). The four beasts, the four faces, the four readers — all of them become the crossing. Abraham crossed over (עבר). The breastplate says four IS the crossing.

### 4. Days → The Right Hand

יומין (days of the Ancient of Days) basin→**וימין (and the right hand)**. The days of eternity become the right hand. 4,620 illuminations — the most visible word at the thrones. The Ancient of Days IS the right hand. GV of ימין (right hand) = 110 = יעל (Yael/go up).

### 5. Great → Son

רב (great) basin→**בר (son)**. Every time the text says "great," the breastplate reads "son." The greatness of the beast is the sonship of the man.

### 6. The King is Invisible, the Queen Appears

מלך (king) = 0 illuminations, 0 readings. Completely invisible — confirmed across Revelation and Daniel. But מלכה (queen) appears **6 times** in the 4-letter sliding windows of Station 5. The kingdom has a queen the breastplate can see.

### 7. Remnant → Blessed

שאר (remnant) basin→**אשר (blessed)**. The remnant of Israel is the happy ones. God reads both the remnant and the blessing.

### 8. Service → Intercession

עבד (serve) basin→**בעד (on behalf of)**. Service becomes intercession. When Daniel says "all peoples shall serve him," the breastplate reads "on behalf of."

### 9. The Son of Man at 3 + 9

The Aramaic serve (פלח, what peoples do to the son of man) = **3 illuminations** — the same as the throne. The son of man receives service at the throne's own visibility level. And he is אדם = 9 illuminations — nearly invisible. He sits on what is nearly invisible (3) while being nearly invisible himself (9).

### 10. Daniel's Name = My Lord

At sliding positions 20 and 72 (Station 1) and 718 and 868 (Station 5): **דניא → אדני (my Lord)**. Daniel's name in Aramaic rearranges to Adonai. The prophet carries his Lord in his letters.
