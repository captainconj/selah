## The Breaking

The most important basin walk in the experiment:

**שבר (break)** — 180 illuminations, **720 readings** (= 10 × 72).

Basin → **בשר (flesh / good news)**.

| Reader | Primary reading |
|--------|----------------|
| Aaron | בשר (flesh, 33) |
| God | **בשר (flesh, 41)** |
| Right | בשר (flesh, 27) |
| Left | **בשר (flesh, 42)** |

All four readers agree: **breaking IS flesh.** The stone that breaks the statue IS the incarnation. The Word became flesh. And בשר is also "tidings" — the root of בשורה (gospel / good news). The stone that shatters every idol IS the good news.

720 = 10 × 72. The breaking stamps the breastplate ten times over.

The Aramaic form confirms: **תבר (break, Aramaic) → בתר (between / halve)**. Abraham's covenant of the pieces (Genesis 15) — the cutting between. The stone that breaks IS the covenant.

### Station 2: The Sure Foundation (Isaiah 28:14–22)

**Position 1:** משכן (**tabernacle**). The first 4-letter reading in the foundation passage is the tabernacle. The sure foundation IS the dwelling place.

**Righteousness (צדקה)**: 72 readings — stamps the breastplate.

**Plumb line (משקלת)**: 72 illuminations — lights up the entire breastplate. The measuring instrument and the breastplate have the same count.

**Amen (אמן) GV=91**: fixed point. 91 = triangle(13) = sum of love. But God reads both **אמן** and **מאן (refuse)**. Amen contains refusal.

**Stone (אבן) ×5** in the 3-letter windows — stone surfaces repeatedly in its own passage.

**Servant (עבד) ×5** alongside it. The foundation is built by the servant.

### Station 3: The Stone Without Hands (Daniel 2:31–45)

**Gold (זהב)**: 72 readings — stamps the breastplate. The gold head of the statue is counted by the breastplate.

**Fill (מלא)**: 72 readings — stamps the breastplate. "The stone filled the whole earth." The filling IS the breastplate counting.

**Silver (כסף)**: 1 illumination. Barely visible. The chest of silver barely glows.

**Clay (חסף)**: 1 illumination. The feet of clay barely glow.

**Earth Hebrew (ארץ)**: 0/0 — invisible. The earth the stone fills cannot be seen.

**Hand (יד) GV=14 = gold (זהב) GV=14**. Hand and gold have the same value. The stone cut without HANDS shatters the head of GOLD. Hand = gold. What was absent in the cutting is what falls first.

**Boy/child (ילד) ×8** in the sliding windows. The stone is the child.

**Judge/law (דין) ×12** in the sliding windows. The stone judges.

**The Hand (היד) ×10** in the windows — the hand that was absent dominates.

### Station 4: The Capstone (Zechariah 4:1–10)

**The Stone (האבן) ×4** in 4-letter windows. The capstone names itself.

**Head (ראשה) ×4** — the head of the capstone.

**Seven (השבע) ×5** — "these seven are the eyes of the LORD."

**Spirit (רוח)**: Aaron reads **חור (hole)** ×12, רוח (spirit) ×1. The priest reads HOLE where the text says SPIRIT. Same finding as at the cross (experiment 113). The priest sees the opening. God sees the breath.

**Power (כח)**: 1 illumination. "Not by power" — and power barely glows.

**Might (חיל)**: Aaron reads **חלי (sickness)** ×19 AND חיל (might) ×14. "Not by might" — and the priest reads might as sickness.

**Level/plain (מישר)**: 660 illuminations. Basin → **שמרי (my guardian)**. The mountain that becomes a plain becomes a guardian.

**Eye (עין) GV=130**: 132 illuminations. Basin → **יען (because)**. The eye walks to causation. The seven eyes ARE seven reasons.

## Synthesis

### The Invisible Foundation

The cornerstone is built from invisibility. The king is 0/0. The blessed one is 0/0. The corner walks to null. The rejection walks to null. The face (which shares the corner's root) is ghost zone. The earth the stone fills is 0/0. The foundation of the house is made of things the breastplate cannot see.

And yet the stone itself — אבן — is a fixed point. All four readers see it. It does not move. It holds. The invisible king, the invisible blessed one, the invisible corner — they all converge on a stone that every reader can see and that nothing can move.

### The Father-Son Stone

אבן = א + בן. Father + son. GV = 53 = garden. The stone IS the father-son relationship. The stone IS the garden. Eden is a rock. The foundation of the house is the relationship between father and son, which is also the garden from which everything grows.

### The Breaking Is the Good News

שבר → בשר. 720 readings = 10 × 72. All four readers agree: the breaking is flesh. The stone that shatters the idol IS the Word becoming flesh. And בשר is gospel — good news. The demolition IS the announcement. The breaking of every false image IS the good news.

### The Head Is Happy

ראש → אשר. The head of the corner walks to blessed. Not powerful. Not triumphant. Happy. The cornerstone's deepest identity is joy. And the psalm confirms: "This is the day the LORD has made — let us rejoice." Rejoice → anoint. The day of rejoicing IS the day of the Messiah.

### Build = Altar

בנה = מזבח = 57. What the builders build is an altar. They think they're constructing a building. They're making a place of sacrifice. The rejected stone doesn't fit in the building because it's not a building — it's an altar. The stone they throw away is the one the altar needs.

### Grace They Can Say, Lovingkindness They Cannot

חן (grace) = fixed point. חסד (lovingkindness, GV=72) = ghost zone. "Grace, grace to it!" the builders shout. They can say the short word. The full word — the one that IS the breastplate's count — cannot be spoken. Grace is the name they're allowed. Lovingkindness is the name that names itself and therefore cannot be named.
