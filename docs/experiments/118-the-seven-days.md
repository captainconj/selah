## Cross-Station Patterns

### The Evening-Morning Refrain
Every day ends with the same letter sequence: ויהיערבויהיבקר (and-there-was evening and-there-was morning). The sliding windows at the boundary always produce:
- העי (the Eye/Ai) — the ruin, the shovel's anagram
- עיר (city) — the city emerges from every evening
- רבע (quarter) — the evening divides
- קבר (grave) — morning is read as GRAVE by the sliding window. Every dawn carries a burial.

### Per-Head Divergences

## Synthesis

### Blessing Is Invisible, Goodness Holds

God blesses three times in the creation week (Day 5, Day 6, Day 7). And blessing is invisible — 0/0, every time, in every experiment. But good (טוב) is a fixed point, 168 readings, visible primarily to God. God can see good. God cannot speak blessing. The seeing and the speaking are different operations.

### The Lamb Is in the Mandate

כבש appears on Day 6 as "subdue." The same word as "lamb." And it walks to שכב (lie down). The dominion mandate — "fill the earth and subdue it" — IS the lamb that lies down. God reads שכב(20). The command to rule IS the command to be sacrificed. This is not interpretation. This is the basin walk of the word itself.

### Image = Throne = Unspeakable

צלם (image) = 3 illuminations = throne signature. It walks to null. Man is made in the image of God, and the image has the same glow as the throne (כסא=3), and neither can be spoken. The thing man is made in the image OF cannot be named by the oracle. It can only glow.

### The Ram in the Name of God

האיל (the ram) appears 38 times across creation — because every time אלהי (Elohi-, "God-of") appears, the cherubim reverse it to the ram. The name of God read backward IS the ram. Not once. Thirty-eight times. The substitute animal is encoded in the divine name as a reading direction.

### Separation Is Loneliness

בדל → לבד. The fundamental creative act — dividing, separating, distinguishing — walks to "alone." And לבדי (alone) appears 9 times in the 4-letter sliding windows. Creation is differentiation, and differentiation is solitude. "It is not good for man to be alone" is the answer to the loneliness that creation itself produces.

### Evening Harms, Morning Holds

ערב → ברע (evening → in harm). בקר → בקר (morning → self). Every day begins with destruction and ends with a fixed point. The evening dissolves. The morning crystallizes. And God reads every morning as lightning (ברק). The new day strikes.

### Creation Ends Where It Begins

Position 128 of Day 7: בראש (in the head/beginning). The last station's sliding window produces the first word of Torah. The week is a circle. The sabbath returns to the beginning.
