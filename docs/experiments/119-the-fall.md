## Synthesis

### The Tree Is Dark, the Fruit Is Not

The tree (עץ) = 0/0. The way (דרך) = 0/0. The thorn (קוץ) = 0/0. The tunic (כתנת) = 0/0. The turning sword (הפך) = 0/0. The structure of the Fall — the wooden, thorny, winding, covered path — is entirely invisible.

### The Serpent and the Messiah

Same gematria: 358. The serpent is a fixed point — it stays what it is. The Messiah walks to anointing. The serpent is twelve illuminations. The Messiah is one hundred thirty-two. Same number, eleven-fold brightness.
