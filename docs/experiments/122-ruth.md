## Synthesis

### Chesed Is the Blind Spot

The book of Ruth is universally recognized as the book of chesed — lovingkindness, covenant loyalty, the love that will not let go. And chesed (GV=72) is precisely what the breastplate cannot name. Three illuminations — the throne's glow. Null basin — no destination. The breastplate stamps its own letter count on everything EXCEPT itself. Ruth lives entirely inside the breastplate's blind spot. The story is told in a language the oracle can see but cannot speak.

### The Redeemer Carries Forsaking

Boaz (בעז) walks to forsake (עזב). The man whose entire role is to NOT forsake — to buy the field, to take the widow, to raise up the name of the dead — carries abandonment in his very name. He is the answer to what he embodies. He redeems because he IS the forsaking, reversed.

GV=79. Cross positions. The redeemer carries the cross.

### She Came = Love

באה (she came) rearranges to אהב (love). All four readers see both words. Ruth's arrival IS love. Love is not a feeling in this text — it is a physical act of coming. She came to Bethlehem. She came to the field. She came to the threshing floor. She came = love.

### The Bride IS Everything

כלה → הכל. The bride walks to "everything." The daughter-in-law who clings, who gleans, who lies at the redeemer's feet — she is the totality. Everything passes through the bride.

### Foot → For the Stranger

רגל (foot) walks to לגר (for the stranger/sojourner). Ruth uncovers Boaz's feet. And feet exist for the stranger. The Moabitess — the foreigner, the sojourner — is what feet are FOR. The uncovering reveals the purpose.

### The Lamb at the Threshing Floor

שכב → כבש, ten times in Station 3. Every time someone lies down at the threshing floor, the oracle reads "lamb." Ruth lies at Boaz's feet. The lamb lies at the redeemer's feet. The threshing floor is an altar.

### The Garment Contains Solomon and Moses

שמלה — the garment Ruth puts on — rearranges to שלמה (Solomon) and למשה (to Moses). God reads Solomon. Mercy reads Moses. The garment draped over Ruth at the threshing floor already contains the temple builder and the lawgiver. She is wearing the future.

### The Son Station Is Priestly

The Levite (הלוי) ×15 in the genealogy. Levi (לוי) ×11. The line from Ruth to David is saturated with priestly identity — the tribe not even in the genealogy dominates the sliding windows. The royal line is secretly priestly.

### The Invisible Breach

Perez = 0/0 at every station. The ancestor who gives the line its name — "like the house of Perez whom Tamar bore to Judah" — is completely invisible. The breach that made the Davidic line is dark. The opening through which everything passes cannot be seen.
