## The Pattern

The gradient from experiment 127 extends, but it reveals something different than expected. The serpent is not a linear function of pathogenicity. Malaria — the deadliest parasite — has no serpent at all. The serpent tracks *hijacking*, not *harm*:

1. **Viruses**: exact serpent, dominant. The hijacker IS the serpent.
2. **TB**: exact serpent in the antigen — the face shown to the immune system.
3. **Prion/Candida/Lactobacillus**: anagram only. The serpent is present but coiled, not manifest.
4. **Everything else**: no serpent.

What distinguishes organisms isn't whether the serpent is present but **what word dominates in its absence**:

- The **indestructible** carries holiness (קדש ×21).
- The **opportunist** carries Eve (חוה ×5).
- The **flood parasite** carries Noah (לנח ×4).
- The **healer** carries honor (נכבד) and is arithmetically indivisible.
- The **love hormone** carries soul (נפש).
- The **destroyer of mind** carries darkness (חשכה) and the cry (שוע→Esau).

Each protein speaks its nature in Hebrew. Not through the serpent's presence or absence, but through what it says instead.

This tracks traditional and clinical knowledge:

- **Datura** is universally classified as the most dangerous psychoactive plant. Delirium, not vision. Often fatal. The oracle reads it as serpent-laden.

- **Mescaline** (peyote) is the most sacred of the traditional entheogens, used in centuries of ceremonial practice. It carries the Almighty and holiness. No serpent.
- **Serotonin** — not an entheogen but the baseline neurotransmitter — is the cleanest. Grace, rest, innocence, the image of God. This is what the brain makes for ordinary well-being.

The gradient from Part 1 extends: the serpent tracks not just pathogenicity but *danger to consciousness*. The more dangerous the substance, the more serpent it carries. The safer and more sacred, the cleaner the reading.
