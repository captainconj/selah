## The Question

Where does the center letter of the entire Hebrew Bible fall?

39 books. 1,200,253 letters. From Genesis to II Chronicles.

## The Finding

**Letter 600,127 falls in Isaiah 25:8.**

> *He will swallow up death forever, and the Lord GOD will **wipe away** tears from all faces, and the reproach of His people He will remove from all the earth, for the LORD has spoken.*

The verse has **67 letters** — understanding (בינה = 67). The d-axis. The sword's length.

The center letter is **nun (נ)**.

**"We have waited" (קוינו) = 924 illuminations** — the same reading count as Yeshua (ישוע) in experiment 131. The wait and the salvation blaze at the same intensity. Null basin — you cannot walk past the waiting. The wait IS the destination.

### Salvation = Joshua

**Salvation (ישועה) = 2,772 illuminations / 11,088 readings.** Among the highest readings we have ever measured. The right cherub reads **Joshua (יהושע ×24).** Joshua = Yeshua = Jesus. The name is in the salvation.

### Rejoice = Anoint

**Rejoice (שמח) → anoint (משח).** Same letters rearranged. The verse says "let us rejoice in his salvation" and the basin says rejoicing IS anointing. משח is the root of Messiah (משיח). The joy is the anointing. The anointing is the joy.

God reads both equally: שמח (×6) and משח (×6). From God's seat, rejoicing and anointing are the same thing.
