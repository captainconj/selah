# Experiment 143e: Host Enrichment Analysis

*Which Torah words host fibers more than chance predicts? Not the common words. The rare ones. The meaningful ones.*

Type: `exploration`
State: `mixed`

---

## Method

Baseline: each Torah word covers a fraction of the 305,474 letter-positions. את covers 1.71%. יהוה covers 1.97%. A random process would land in each word proportional to its coverage.

Enrichment ratio = (observed host fraction) / (expected from letter coverage). Ratio > 1 means over-represented. Ratio < 1 means under-represented.

7,100 fiber-letters from 6 search words (תורה כבש שלום אמת חיים ברית), non-surface only, canonical 4D [7 50 13 67].

## Reproduction

```clojure
(require '[selah.search :as s] '[selah.fiber :as f])
(s/build!)

;; Covenant ceremony
(let [hits (s/find-word [7 50 13 67] "ברית")]
  (f/print-fiber (first (filter #(and (= (:skip %) 68)
                                      (some (fn [tw] (and tw (= (:word tw) "מצבה")))
                                            (:torah-words %))) hits))))

;; Enrichment analysis
(let [idx (s/index)
      n (double (:n idx))
      cov (reduce (fn [m w] (update m (:word w) (fnil + 0) (- (:end w) (:start w))))
                  {} (:words idx))
      hosts (atom {}) total (atom 0)]
  (doseq [w ["תורה" "כבש" "שלום" "אמת" "חיים" "ברית"]]
    (doseq [h (f/non-surface (s/find-word [7 50 13 67] w {:max-results 500}))]
      (doseq [tw (:torah-words h)]
        (when tw (swap! total inc) (swap! hosts update (:word tw) (fnil inc 0))))))
  (let [t (double @total)]
    (doseq [[w c] (take 30 (sort-by #(/ (double (val %))
                                         (/ (double (get cov (key %) 1)) n))
                                     > @hosts))]
      (when (>= c 3)
        (println w c (format "%.1fx" (/ (/ (double c) t) (/ (double (get cov w 1)) n))))))))
```
