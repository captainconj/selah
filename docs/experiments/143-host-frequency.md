## Method

Search Hebrew words along every direction in the canonical 4D space [7 × 50 × 13 × 67]. For each non-surface hit (skip ≠ ±1), record which Torah word each search-letter lands inside. Count. Rank. No curated list. Let the geometry speak.

The index: 304,850 letters, 5,846 verses, 80,474 Torah words tracked with letter-level positions.

40 unique directions in 4D. Each direction plus its reverse. Non-surface = every direction except the natural reading.

## Reproduction

```clojure
(require '[selah.search :as s] '[selah.fiber :as f])
(s/build!)

;; Truth fiber: garden to expulsion
(let [hits (s/find-word [7 50 13 67] "אמת")]
  (f/print-fiber (first (filter #(= (:skip %) 805)
                                (filter #(some (fn [tw] (and tw (= (:word tw) "האדם")))
                                               (:torah-words %)) hits)))))

;; Covenant fiber: oath to anointed
(let [hits (s/find-word [7 50 13 67] "ברית")]
  (f/print-fiber (first (filter #(some (fn [tw] (and tw (= (:word tw) "המשיח")))
                                        (:torah-words %))
                                (f/non-surface hits)))))

;; Host frequency
(doseq [w ["תורה" "כבש" "שלום" "יהוה" "אמת" "חיים"]]
  (let [hits (s/find-word [7 50 13 67] w)
        ns (f/non-surface hits)
        hosts (frequencies (mapcat #(map :word (remove nil? (:torah-words %))) ns))]
    (println w (count ns) "non-surface," (count hosts) "unique hosts")
    (doseq [[word cnt] (take 10 (sort-by val > hosts))]
      (println "  " word cnt))))
```
