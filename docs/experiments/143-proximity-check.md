# Experiment 143ar: Proximity Check

*Some findings are proximity — short-range axes sampling nearby content. Some genuinely reach across chapters. The distinction matters. The discipline catches what the narrative obscured.*

## The Issue

The love axis (skip=67) moves only 1-2 verses per step. A 7-step reading stays within the same chapter. When the Shema (Deut 6:4) reads "on the way, in your gates" along the love axis, those words are in Deut 6:7-9 — just 3-5 verses away. Of course nearby verses contain related content.

Genesis 1:1 "retelling creation" on the love axis — all 7 steps stay within Genesis 1:1-10. That is not the geometry selecting creation language from across the Torah. It is sampling from the same passage.

## What Survives

The falling diagonal (skip=804) genuinely spans chapters. The command→ate→garments→wandering reading crosses 3 chapters — Genesis 2:16 to 4:14. The Torah places these host words at 804-letter intervals across the fall narrative. That is a structural fact.

The jubilee walk (50 stations across 30 chapters of Genesis) is genuine reach. The sabbath at station 2, Abel at station 5, the covering at station 13, the righteous at station 25. These span the breadth of Genesis.

The completeness axis (7 letters across the whole Torah) is maximum reach. בתוך (in the midst) spelling across 5 books.

The axis centers (each in a different part of the Torah) are genuine. The jubilee center at the verse asking for 50 righteous — that is structural.

The love-axis readings should be reported as proximity observations, not as structural findings. They are still interesting — it matters that nearby content aligns — but they do not carry the same weight as findings that span chapters.

---

## The Lesson (Again)

The discipline. When something sounds beautiful, check how far it reaches. Short-range axes find nearby content. Long-range axes find distant content. Both are observations. Only the long-range readings demonstrate structural selection across the text.

The fall diagonal is real. The jubilee walk is real. The completeness axis is real. The love-axis self-readings are proximity.
