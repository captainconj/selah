# Experiment 143p: The Structural Words

*Choose finds the altar. Guard finds the breath and the branch. The pillar finds the lamb. Atone finds the mercy seat. Holy finds righteousness. Command finds YHWH. The aleph-tav finds itself. Each structural word finds what it structurally serves.*

---

## Choose — בחר (GV=210)

878 non-surface hits. The largest basin attractor — choosing is the biggest pool.

**Top hosts:** אשר (79), בני (41), ויאמר (31), **המזבח (the altar, 26)**, הארץ (25), ישראל (25), **אחד (one, 24)**, דבר (21), **אחרי (after, 20)**.

**Choosing finds the altar.** The place of decision. The word most associated with the sacrificial system. And it finds **one** (אחד, GV=13=love) 24 times. Choosing finds oneness. The act of choosing IS the act of becoming one — selecting, from many, the one.

**Richest fiber:** [בני → רוחי → אחרי] — "sons of → my spirit → after me." Genesis 6:2-3. The sons of God and the limit placed on the spirit. Choosing starts at the boundary between divine and human.

**The priests:** At Exodus 28:1, בחר passes through **בניו → ויקח → אשר** (his sons → he took → which). The choosing of Aaron and his sons for the priesthood. The word finds its own action.

## Guard — שמר (GV=540)

| Letter | Host Word | Verse |
|--------|-----------|-------|
| ש | **נשמת** (breath of) | Genesis 2:7 |
| מ | **ויצמח** (and he caused to grow) | Genesis 2:9 |

**Genesis 2:7** — "God formed man of the dust of the ground and breathed into his nostrils the **breath** of life."

Guard starts at the breath of life, passes through the growing, and arrives at evil. The guard is needed because of what grows. The instruction to guard comes after the planting.

## Reproduction

```clojure
(require '[selah.search :as s] '[selah.fiber :as f])
(s/build!)

;; Any structural word
(let [hits (s/find-word [7 50 13 67] "קדש")]
  (doseq [h (take 5 (sort-by :torah-word-count > (f/non-surface hits)))]
    (f/print-fiber h)))

;; Anagram enrichment for any pair
(let [idx (s/index) n (double (:n idx))
      cov (fn [w] (double (reduce + (map #(- (:end %) (:start %)) 
                                          (filter #(= (:word %) w) (:words idx))))))]
  ;; test search-word → anagram-host
  (let [hits (s/find-word [7 50 13 67] "ראש" {:max-results 500})
        ns (f/non-surface hits)
        total (double (* 3 (count ns)))
        hosts (frequencies (mapcat #(map :word (remove nil? (:torah-words %))) ns))
        obs (double (get hosts "אשר" 0))
        exp (* (/ (cov "אשר") n) total)]
    (println (format "ראש→אשר: obs=%.0f exp=%.1f ratio=%.1fx" obs exp (/ obs exp)))))
```
