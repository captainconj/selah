## The Arc

The peg traces the Torah's structural narrative:

1. **The fall** — Cain, the first violence
2. **The corruption** — the thoughts of man before the flood
3. **The covenant sign** — circumcision, the aleph-tav
4. **The defiance** — the midwives who chose life
5. **The visitation** — the burning bush, "I have visited"
6. **The slavery** — beaten for straw, the lowest point
7. **The building** — the tabernacle itself, sockets and cubits

From murder to building. From the fall to the house. The peg holds the narrative taut between Cain's violence and the tabernacle's construction.

All at skip=66, walking along [0,0,+1,-1]: love ascending, understanding descending. The peg walks toward love while releasing understanding. It does not need to understand. It needs to hold.
