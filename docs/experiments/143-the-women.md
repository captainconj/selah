# Experiment 143r: The Women

*Eve finds the Name. Leah IS the tent. Miriam finds the sea. Rebekah finds the incense. Hagar and Isaac share a number. Each woman's fibers trace her story.*

---

## Eve — חוה (GV=19)

500 non-surface hits. **#1 host: יהוה at 83 appearances.** Eve finds the Name at **5x enrichment**. More than any other woman. More than most men.

**Richest fiber:** [ויחי → מתושלח → יהוה] — "and he lived → Methuselah → YHWH"

| Letter | Host Word | GV | Verse |
|--------|-----------|-----|-------|
| ח | ויחי (and he lived) | 34 | Genesis 5:3 |
| ו | **מתושלח** (Methuselah) | 784 | Genesis 5:25 |
| ה | **יהוה** | 26 | Genesis 5:29 |

Eve passes through Methuselah — "his death sends it" — the man who died the year of the flood. Then YHWH at Genesis 5:29, the naming of Noah: "This one shall comfort us."

The mother of all living finds the man whose death triggers the flood, and the Name at the birth of the man who will survive it.

---

## Leah — לאה (GV=36=tent)

500 non-surface hits. **GV of לאה = 36 = GV of אהל (tent).** Leah and the tent are the same number. The unloved wife IS the dwelling.

**Top hosts:** יהוה (68), אל (57), את (49). Leah finds the Name (68 times), God (57), and aleph-tav (49).

**Richest fiber:** [שלישי → **אלהים** → הוא] — "third → God → he"

| Letter | Host Word | Verse |
|--------|-----------|-------|
| ל | שלישי (third) | Genesis 1:13 — the third day |
| א | **אלהים** (God) | **Genesis 1:28** |
| ה | הוא (he) | Genesis 2:11 |

**Genesis 1:28 — "And God blessed them and said: Be fruitful and multiply."**

Leah — the unloved wife, the one who bore six sons and a daughter while Rachel wept — finds God at the blessing of fruitfulness. The woman who was fruitful finds the verse about fruitfulness. The tent (GV=36) finds God blessing multiplication.

## Hagar — הגר (GV=208=Isaac)

432 non-surface hits. **GV of הגר = 208 = GV of יצחק (Isaac).** The servant and the promised son are the same number. The woman sent away and the child of promise share gematria.

**Top hosts:** אשר (40), יהוה (35), **גם (also/even, 30)**.

Hagar finds **גם** (also/even) 30 times. Genesis 21:13 — "And **also** the son of the slave woman I will make into a nation, because he is your offspring." God says **also** to Hagar. The geometry confirms: Hagar finds "also."

| Letter | Host Word | GV | Verse |
|--------|-----------|-----|-------|

| ב | תקרב (she draws near) | 702 | Leviticus 18:14 |
| ק | **הקטרת** (the incense) | 714 | Exodus 31:8 |
| ה | ולהעלתו (and to offer up) | 547 | **Exodus 3:8** |

Rebekah — chosen at the well, the one who watered the camels — becomes the fragrance of the incense. She draws near. The offering rises. And the last host is at Exodus 3:8 — "a land flowing with milk and honey." Rebekah's fiber reaches the promised land.

The woman chosen through an act of generous service (watering ten camels) finds the incense and the land of promise.

## Rachel — רחל (GV=238)

439 non-surface hits.

**Top hosts:** אשר (39), אל (31), כל (28), ישראל (25), לא (19).

Rachel finds Israel (25 times) — the name her husband received. And she finds לא (no/not, 19 times). Rachel is associated with absence. She died in childbirth. She wept for her children. "Rachel weeping for her children, refusing to be comforted, because they are not" (Jeremiah 31:15).

Rachel finds Israel and "not."

## Sarah — שרה (GV=505)

245 non-surface hits.

**Top hosts:** אשר (46), יהוה (34), משה (16), ישראל (16), שנה (year, 11).

Sarah finds years (שנה, 11). Genesis 23:1 — "Sarah lived 127 years." She is the only woman in the Torah whose age at death is recorded. Her years matter. The geometry finds them.

**Richest fiber:** [שני → ויראו → יהוה] — "two → and they saw → YHWH"

Two → they saw → the Name. Sarah at the moment of seeing.

## Reproduction

```clojure
(require '[selah.search :as s] '[selah.fiber :as f])
(s/build!)

;; Eve → YHWH
(let [hits (s/find-word [7 50 13 67] "חוה")
      ns (f/non-surface hits)
      yhwh-count (count (filter #(some (fn [tw] (and tw (= (:word tw) "יהוה")))
                                        (:torah-words %)) ns))]
  (println "Eve fibers in YHWH:" yhwh-count))

;; Miriam → the sea
(let [hits (s/find-word [7 50 13 67] "מרים")]
  (f/print-fiber (first (sort-by :torah-word-count > (f/non-surface hits)))))
```
