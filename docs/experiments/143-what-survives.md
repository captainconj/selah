## What Is Honestly Uncertain

- **Enrichment findings** (pillar 8.1x, menorah 10.8x, wisdom 21.5x): These control for word frequency but NOT for letter overlap. A full control would compute expected enrichment from shared letters alone. Not yet done.
- **Anagram attraction** (serpent→breastplate 27x): By definition these share all letters. The enrichment is partly tautological for anagram pairs. The non-anagram host findings need the letter-overlap control.
- **Famous verse readings at skip=804**: Spanning 2-3 chapters is genuine reach, but the fall diagonal's host words could be checked against a null model (random skip of similar size).

## What Does NOT Need Correction

- The Torah having **304,850 letters** factoring as **2×5²×7×13×67** is arithmetic.
- The axis sum being **137** is arithmetic.
- The literal center being the **altar** is a positional fact.
- The scroll loop spelling **לב** (heart) is a positional fact.
- Being words dominating all directions is a counting fact.
- Each axis having a **different vocabulary** is a counting fact.

These are properties of the text. Not interpretations. Not claims. Facts.
