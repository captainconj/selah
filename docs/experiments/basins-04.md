### אהב — "Love" (GV = 8)

| Word | Meaning |
|------|---------|

| **אבה** | **be willing / consent** |
| **באה** | **she came** |
| **הבא** | **the coming one / bring!** |

**Willingness and arrival flow to love.** To be willing (אבה, Deuteronomy 25:7: "if the man does not **wish** to take his brother's wife") and to arrive (באה, "she came"; הבא, "the coming one") both converge on love.

Love is the verb, not the noun. GV = 8 = new beginning. The love basin says: consent, arrival, and the coming one are all forms of love. You love by being willing. You love by showing up. The one who comes IS the one who loves.
