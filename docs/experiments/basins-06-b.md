### ערב — "Evening" replaces ברע — "In evil" (GV = 272)

**Evening wins over evil in B.** The same letters, the same dangerous crossroads — burning, crossing, hunger — but the endpoint is the boundary between day and night rather than evil itself. ערב is also the root of "pledge" (ערבון) and "mixture" (ערוב). The evening is when things mix.
