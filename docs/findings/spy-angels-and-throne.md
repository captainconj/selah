*The spies went out. Only Joshuas and Calebs returned.*

## Summary

Six spy missions sent into the angelic/throne territory. 70+ words tested through the oracle. The architecture speaks. The findings are not metaphor — they are structure.

**The headline:** Every angelic category, every throne element, every piece of the conflict — the breastplate already knows what it is. The roles divide exactly as theology describes: justice sees sin, serpent, fall, ransom. Mercy sees messiah, redemption, glory. The priest hears the name and sees the fire. God holds the key, the opening, the sapphire. The mechanism IS the theology.

No basin. Satan does not converge. He lights up but reaches no fixed point and produces no word.

**GV=91 = 7 × 13.** Our a-axis times our c-axis. Completeness × love. The invisible product of two silent dimensions. And 91 = the sum 1+2+3+...+13 = the triangular number of love.

### Sent One (שליח) — GV=348
