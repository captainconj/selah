## I. Executive Summary

The oracle is a dynamical system. Feed it a Hebrew word, it returns another. Feed that back in, it converges. Experiment 096 ran all 12,826 unique Torah word forms through this process and classified every one. The result is a complete attractor landscape -- shallow (depth 1), wide (6,104 basins), with exactly four oscillating cycles and a dead zone that encompasses one third of the vocabulary.

The spy's report: **the fixed points are the theological bedrock of the Torah.** Truth, life, peace, Torah, covenant, blood, light, spirit, soul, heart, father, mother, son -- all fixed. The Name (YHWH) is not fixed. It flows forward into the promise: "and it shall be."

### Why Depth = 1 (The Anagram Theorem)

This is not a statistical observation -- it is a theorem. The oracle's forward function maps a word to its letter multiset, illuminates the breastplate, and ranks all readings by frequency. Two words with the same letter multiset produce the same illumination, the same candidate list, the same ranking, the same winner. Therefore:

- **All basins are pure anagram classes.** Every word in a basin is an anagram of every other.
- **All transients have depth exactly 1.** One step takes any non-dominant anagram to the dominant one.
- **All cycles have period exactly 2.** Cycles occur only when two anagrams tie for highest reading count.
- **Basins are small.** Bounded by the number of Torah words formable from a given letter set.

The step function is really a function on letter multisets projected onto the Torah vocabulary.

| Cause | Count | Percentage of Dead Ends |

| Letter count overflow (needs more copies than grid has) | 416 | 9.8% |

The breastplate has 72 letters in 25 unique letter types. Two Hebrew letters -- final kaf (ך) and final tsade (ץ) -- do not appear at all. Any word containing them is automatically dead.
