### Connection Types
- **PREDICTS** (-->): Finding A was predicted by or led to finding B
- **VALIDATES** (==>): Finding A provides independent evidence for finding B
- **DEEPENS** (<->): Finding A reveals new structure within finding B
- **CONTRADICTS** (X-->): Finding A challenges or kills finding B
