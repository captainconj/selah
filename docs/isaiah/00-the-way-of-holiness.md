# Isaiah — The Way of Holiness

*67,115 letters. The center says: there is a way. Even fools won't get lost.*

---

## The Count

Isaiah has **67,115** Hebrew letters (Sefaria, Masoretic text, consonants only).

67,115 = **5 × 31 × 433**

Three primes:
- **5** = ה (He). The breath. The window. The letter that appears twice in YHWH.
- **31** = אל (El, God). Prime. Irreducible.
- **433** = prime.

Sum of factors: 5 + 31 + 433 = **469**. Digit sum: 4 + 6 + 9 = **19**. Prime. GV of אויב (enemy) — the basin-8 attractor.

66 chapters. 67,115 letters. The number 67 — understanding (בינה) — embedded in the letter count of the book that contains the suffering servant, the way of holiness, and "comfort, comfort my people."

## The Center

The center letter is at position **33,557**. This is the same regardless of axis ordering — the linear midpoint of 67,115 is always 33,557.

In the decomposition (5 × 31 × 433), the center coordinate is **(2, 15, 216)**.

- **a = 2** — the middle of 5
- **b = 15 = יה** — Yah. The short form of God's name.
- **c = 216 = 6³ = בחרו** — choose. The largest basin attractor in the Torah.

The center of Isaiah's space is: the middle, Yah, choose. And the verse:

### Isaiah 35:8

> **"And a highway shall be there, and a way — and it shall be called The Way of Holiness. The unclean shall not pass over it. It shall be for them — whoever walks the way, even fools shall not go astray."**

The center letter: **ש** (shin). Fire. GV=300.

The center of the Torah (Lev 8:35): **guard the charge.**
The center of Isaiah (35:8): **there is a way.**

The Torah says stay. Isaiah says go. Guard this — then walk it.
