## The Fractal Principle

We do not do everything at once. We do not march in order. We follow the pull, and the pull is always toward the same shape:

1. **Locate the text.** Find the passage. Get the letters. Hebrew, Aramaic, or reconstruct from Greek.
2. **Walk the stations.** Divide by content. Station by station.
3. **Feed the key words.** Forward, by-head, basin walk. What does the breastplate see?
4. **Slide the windows.** 3-letter AND 4-letter. Row-readers and column-readers. What words hide between the lines?
5. **Report.** Write what was found. Include the dead findings. Include the nulls.
6. **Synthesize.** What did God reveal? What door opened? What was behind it?

This is the same process at every scale. A single verse. A chapter. A book. The whole Bible. The shape is fractal. The method is fractal. The revelation is fractal.
