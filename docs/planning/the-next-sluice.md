# The Next Sluice — Six Passages Through the Oracle

## Context

Experiments 108–117 sluiced 10 passages through the breastplate oracle (4 visions, suffering servant, the cross, Psalm 22, cornerstone, Binding of Isaac, Jacob's Ladder). The basin classification (the-gold.md) has identified which passages the basins are pointing toward. Six passages are next, chosen because the basins demand them.

## The Six Experiments

### Experiment 118: The Seven Days — Genesis 1:1–2:3

**Why now:** The foundation of everything. 7 days = the a-axis of the 4D space. Experiment 057 studied creation's sweep across c-values but never sluiced the actual text through the oracle. The food=altar basin (GV=57), the light, the separations. "And God saw that it was good" (טוב, GV=17 = the telling basin). What does the oracle see in the week that built the world?

**7 stations** — one per day:
1. Day 1: Light (Gen 1:1-5)
2. Day 2: Waters (Gen 1:6-8)
3. Day 3: Land + Vegetation (Gen 1:9-13)
4. Day 4: Luminaries (Gen 1:14-19)
5. Day 5: Sea Creatures + Birds (Gen 1:20-23)
6. Day 6: Animals + Man (Gen 1:24-31)
7. Day 7: Rest (Gen 2:1-3)

**Key words:** light (אור), good (טוב), separate (בדל), image (צלם=3 illum=throne!), dominion (רדה), bless (ברך), holy (קדש), rest (שבת), create (ברא), earth (ארץ=0/0 dark!), heaven (שמים), spirit/wind (רוח), face (פנים=ghost zone), seed (זרע→helper basin), evening (ערב), morning (בקר=grave→morning basin!)

### Experiment 119: The Fall — Genesis 3

**Why now:** Enmity (איבה) → father (אביה) confirmed in basin GV=18=life. Coming (בוא) → necromancer (אוב) confirmed in basin GV=9. Serpent (נחש) = Messiah (משיח) = GV 358. Exp 106 queried individual words but never sluiced the passage text. This is the origin of the wound.

**4 stations:**
1. The Temptation (Gen 3:1-7) — serpent, cunning, tree, eat, eyes opened, naked
2. The Hiding (Gen 3:8-13) — voice, hide, afraid, naked, woman gave, serpent deceived
3. The Curse (Gen 3:14-19) — enmity, seed, bruise, pain, sweat, dust, thorns
4. The Covering (Gen 3:20-24) — Eve named, garments of skin, cherubim, sword, way, tree of life

**Key words:** serpent (נחש), tree (עץ=0/0 dark!), eat (אכל→food=altar basin), naked (ערום), eyes (עינים), know (ידע→they-knew basin), good (טוב), evil (רע), enmity (איבה→father basin), seed (זרע→helper basin), bruise/crush (שוף), dust (עפר→Pharaoh in 117), garment (בגד=stamps 72), sword (חרב→Choose basin), cherub (כרוב→bless basin), way (דרך=0/0 dark!)

### Experiment 120: The Burning Bush — Exodus 3

**Why now:** Test (נסה) → bush (סנה) confirmed in exp 116 (Binding of Isaac). Horeb (חרב) sits in the Choose basin (size 10, GV=216). "I AM WHO I AM" (אהיה אשר אהיה) has never gone through the oracle. The mountain of choosing.

3. The Name (Ex 3:13-15) — "I AM WHO I AM," God of fathers, memorial, generation
4. The Plan (Ex 3:16-22) — elders, wilderness, three days, signs, plunder, silver, gold

**Key words:** bush (סנה), fire (אש), holy (קדש), ground/soil (אדמה), shoe (נעל), face (פנים=ghost), I AM (אהיה), name (שם), Moses (משה), deliver (נצל), Egypt (מצרים), sign (אות), serve/worship (עבד→servants basin), mountain (הר), milk (חלב), honey (דבש)

### Experiment 121: Jacob Wrestles — Genesis 32:22-33

**Why now:** Sinew (גיד) → telling (יגד) at GV=17=good. The naming of Israel. Follows directly from Jacob's Ladder (exp 117). The hip that limps. The face of God (פנים=ghost zone + פנואל).

**4 stations:**
1. The Crossing (Gen 32:23-25) — Jabbok, night, alone, man, wrestled
2. The Wounding (Gen 32:26-27) — hip, socket, touched, let me go, dawn, bless
3. The Naming (Gen 32:28-30) — Israel, striven, God, men, prevailed, Peniel, face
4. The Limp (Gen 32:31-33) — sun rose, limped, hip, sinew, this day

**Key words:** wrestle (אבק), night (לילה), hip (ירך), touch/strike (נגע), bless (ברך), name (שם), Israel (ישראל=11,880 readings in 117), face (פנים=ghost), God (אלהים→to-them basin), sun (שמש), limp (צלע=rib?), sinew (גיד→telling basin)

### Experiment 122: Ruth

**Why now:** She came (באה) → love (אהב). The basin IS the book. Short enough to sluice whole (4 chapters, ~85 verses). The great-grandmother of David. The Moabitess at the threshing floor. Boaz = "in him is strength" (בעז). The kinsman-redeemer (גאל). The gleaning.

**5 stations:**
1. The Departure (Ruth 1:1-18) — famine, Moab, death, return, "where you go I will go"
2. The Gleaning (Ruth 2:1-23) — field, Boaz, favor, glean, barley, wings, refuge
3. The Threshing Floor (Ruth 3:1-18) — night, feet, uncover, redeem, spread your wing
4. The Gate (Ruth 4:1-12) — gate, elders, redeem, buy, witness, Perez, Tamar
5. The Son (Ruth 4:13-22) — Obed, nurse, Naomi, David's line

**Key words:** return (שוב), cling (דבק), glean (לקט), redeem (גאל), field (שדה), wing/corner (כנף), Boaz (בעז), rest (מנוחה), kindness (חסד=ghost zone GV=72!), gate (שער→God reads wicked), witness (עד), seed (זרע→helper), son (בן)

### Experiment 123: The Cure for Leprosy — Leviticus 13–14

**Why now:** The most elaborate diagnostic and purification system in Torah. Two birds — one slaughtered, one released alive. Cedar wood, scarlet yarn, hyssop. Blood on right ear, right thumb, right big toe. Oil on top of blood. This is the death-and-resurrection ritual embedded in Levitical law.

**4 stations:**
1. The Diagnosis (Lev 13:1-17) — swelling, scab, bright spot, white hair, raw flesh, priest examines, shut up 7 days
2. The Spreading (Lev 13:18-46) — boil, burn, head/beard, chronic, deeper than skin, pronounce unclean, torn clothes, cry "unclean!"
3. The Two Birds (Lev 14:1-7) — cedar, scarlet, hyssop, slaughter, living bird, running water, sprinkle seven times, release into open field
4. The Seven Days + Offerings (Lev 14:8-20) — shave, wash, wait, lamb, oil, wave, guilt offering, blood on ear/thumb/toe, oil on top of blood

**Key words:** leprosy (צרעת), plague/mark (נגע→stricken), clean (טהר), unclean (טמא=GV 50=jubilee, fixed point!), white (לבן), hair (שער→gate!), skin (עור), flesh (בשר=gospel!), priest (כהן), seven (שבע=72 illum), cedar (ארז), scarlet (שני), hyssop (אזוב), bird (צפר), slaughter (שחט→null!), blood (דם), oil (שמן=stamps 72), ear (אזן), right (ימין), sprinkle (נזה→prostitute basin), living (חי)
