## Observations

The mercy seat and the veil are **anagrams of each other**. כפרת (kapporet) and פרכת (parokhet) — the same four letters, rearranged. The covering above and the curtain before. The oracle confirms: ask about one, you receive the other.

Their sum: 700 + 700 = 1,400 = 7 x 50 x 4. Four pillars hold the veil (Exodus 26:32). Seven and fifty are the first two axes of the 4D space. The pair encodes the structure.

Only **5 illuminations** — the fewest of any word in these questions. The mercy seat barely lights up on the breastplate. In the reading machine analysis (experiment 084), כפרת is *mute*: the breastplate letters glow but no reader can produce it. The mercy seat cannot name itself. It is the surface on which the blood is sprinkled, the place from which God speaks — but it is not spoken.

כפתר (kaphthor) — the ornamental knob on the menorah (Exodus 25:33). The mercy seat's letters also make the lampstand's decoration. תכפר — "you shall atone." The letters of the mercy seat are the letters of atonement.

700 = 7 x 100. Seven: completeness. One hundred: the fullness of counting. The mercy seat is complete fullness.
