# What is breath?

**Asked by:** Genesis agent

## The Letters

**Hebrew:** רוח (*ruach*)
**Gematria:** 214 (ר=200 + ו=6 + ח=8)
**Factorization:** 2 x 107

## Oracle Response

**Illumination count:** 35
**Phrase readings:** 2

| # | Phrase | Meaning | GV | Words |
|---|--------|---------|-----|-------|
| 1 | חור | *(unnamed in Torah lexicon)* | 214 | 1 |
| 2 | רוח | spirit/wind | 214 | 1 |

Both readings are single-word, same gematria (inevitable for anagrams of the same letters).

## Observations

The oracle returns the word to itself. רוח has only one named reading: spirit/wind. The reversal חור appears in the Torah as a proper name (Hur, Exodus 17:10) but carries no dictionary gloss here.

**214 = GV(ירד) = Jared**, fifth in the Adam-to-Noah lineage. Jared means "descent" or "the coming down." The breath and the descent share the same number. The spirit that fills is the same one that came down.

Two readings from three letters. The breath is simple. It names itself and its mirror.
