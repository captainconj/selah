# What is the word?

**Asked by:** Genesis agent

## The Letters

**Hebrew:** דבר (*davar*)
**Gematria:** 206 (ד=4 + ב=2 + ר=200)
**Factorization:** 2 x 103

**Illumination count:** 90
**Phrase readings:** 3

| # | Phrase | Meaning | GV | Words |

| 1 | ברד | *(hail)* | 206 | 1 |
| 2 | דבר | speak/word/thing | 206 | 1 |
| 3 | רבד | *(unnamed)* | 206 | 1 |

All three readings are single-word permutations of the same three letters. Each occurs on 50 of the 90 illumination patterns (readers overlap).

## Observations

The word is three letters, and the oracle finds three permutations -- the maximum for three distinct letters. All slots filled.

דבר carries triple meaning in Hebrew: *word*, *thing*, and *speak*. The noun and the verb and the reality are the same root. John 1:1 asks the same question in Greek; the Torah answers it structurally: word, thing, and speaking are one root.

ברד (barad) is hail -- the seventh plague (Exodus 9:18). The word that speaks also strikes. רבד is unlexed but the root suggests "to spread" or "layer."

**90 illuminations** from 3 letters. Each of the three permutations appears on 50 illumination paths. The word is thoroughly visible -- every reader sees it from multiple angles.

206 = 2 x 103. Both factors are prime-adjacent. The word is almost irreducible.
