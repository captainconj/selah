# Who is the witness?

**Asked by:** Genesis agent

## The Letters

**Hebrew:** עד (*ed*)
**Gematria:** 74 (ע=70 + ד=4)
**Factorization:** 2 x 37

## Oracle Response

**Illumination count:** 6
**Phrase readings:** 2

| # | Phrase | Meaning | GV | Words |
|---|--------|---------|-----|-------|
| 1 | דע | *(know/knowledge)* | 74 | 1 |
| 2 | עד | until/witness | 74 | 1 |

Both readings occur on all 6 illumination patterns.

## Observations

Two letters. Two readings. The witness and knowledge are the same letters reversed.

עד means both "witness" and "until" -- the one who testifies and the boundary of time. דע is the imperative "know!" (from ידע). To witness is to know, rearranged. The oracle says the witness *is* knowledge, seen from the other side.

**6 illuminations** -- the smallest count so far in this series. The witness is quiet. Few paths lead to it. But every path that reaches it sees both readings.

**74 = 2 x 37.** 37 is the gematria of יחידה (*yechidah*), the singular soul. The witness is doubled singularity. 37 is also a factor of 111 (אלף, *aleph* spelled out) and of 777.

Two letters, two words, one truth: the witness knows.
