**Asked by:** Coach agent

## The Letters

**Hebrew:** חכמה (*chokhmah*)

**Factorization:** 73 is **prime**

## Observations

Wisdom has irreducible gematria. 73 is prime — it cannot be factored, cannot be decomposed into smaller parts. The number itself is indivisible, like the concept it names.

The oracle gives three readings, and two of them ask a question. כח (koach) = power/strength (GV=28=7x4). מה (mah) = "what?" The two-word readings say: **"what is strength?"** and **"strength — what?"** Ask wisdom what it is, and it answers with a question about power. Wisdom is not the possession of strength but the inquiry into it.

6 illuminations from 4 letters. Only 6 ways to light up חכמה on the breastplate — the fewest illuminations of any 4-letter word we've asked about. Wisdom lights up rarely.

73 is the 21st prime. 21 = 7 x 3 = the triangle of 6 (T(6) = 1+2+3+4+5+6 = 21). And 73 itself is the star number: 73 = T(4) + 3 x T(3) = 10 + 3 x 6. The 21st prime where 21 = F(8), the 8th Fibonacci number.

כח = 28 = T(7) = the 7th triangle number = perfect number. Wisdom decomposes into the question about perfection.
