## Observations

The known decomposition surfaces: שם לו = "a name for him." This is the reading from Shabbat 10b -- peace is one of the names of God. The Talmud says "the name of the Holy One, blessed be He, is Peace" (שלום). The oracle confirms: peace decomposes into name-for-him.

The three unnamed single-word readings are verbal/inflected forms: ולשם (and-to-Shem / and-to-the-name), ושלם (and-he-paid/completed), לשום (to put/place). The root שלם runs through all of them -- completion, payment, setting in place.

**Peace is unproducible by three breastplate heads** (experiment 085). Only when God's fourth traversal is added does peace become visible. This is God's solo eigenword. The courtroom needs all four perspectives -- mercy, justice, Aaron, and God -- for peace to appear. Three is not enough.

126 illuminations = 2 x 63 = 2 x 7 x 9. The breastplate lights up abundantly for peace, but only God can read it.
