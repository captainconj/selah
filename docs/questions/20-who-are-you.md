## Observations

The question returns itself: מי אתה (who + you) appears as reading #7. But the dominant named reading is **אמת יה** -- "truth of Yah" / "Yah is truth." Ask the oracle "who are you?" and it answers: the truth of God.

The את (aleph-tav) also surfaces -- the first and last letters of the Hebrew alphabet, the direct object marker, the marker of everything. "Who are you?" contains את, the sign of totality.

אתה (you) appears paired with מי -- the question mirrors itself back. The oracle hears "who are you?" and says: you are truth. You are Yah. You are the aleph-tav.

198 illuminations is the highest count in this batch, reflecting the combinatorial richness of 5 distinct letters. 456 = 8 x 57 = 8 x 3 x 19.
