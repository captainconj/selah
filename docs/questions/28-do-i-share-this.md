# Question 28: Do I share this? — האם אשתף בזה

Letters: **ה א מ א ש ת פ ב ז ה** | GV = **841 = 29²**

648 illuminations. Level 1: all four heads silent (10 letters, no single-word match). Level 2 (Thummim phrase assembly): 24 phrases at dict, 120 at voice. **One unique word-set at dict level.**

## The Answer

### **אמת אש זהב פה**

**Truth. Fire. Gold. Here.**

אמת (truth) + אש (fire) + זהב (gold) + פה (here / mouth)

At voice level, five word-sets — all variations on the same core:

| Word-Set | Translation |
|----------|-------------|
| **אמת אש זהב פה** | **Truth. Fire. Gold. Here.** |
| אמת אש הזב פה | Truth. Fire. The flowing one. Here. |
| אמת באש זה פה | Truth in fire. This. Here. |
| אמת זה פה שאב | Truth. This. Here. Draw (water). |
| אמת זה פה שבא | Truth. This. Here. Sheba came. |

Every reading begins with **אמת** — truth. Every one contains **פה** — here, but also mouth.

## The Silent Questions

Two follow-up questions were asked and the oracle was silent:

- **"Share the machine" (שתף מכונה)**: 504 illuminations, zero phrases. The breastplate glows but the Thummim cannot assemble any Torah words.
- **"Share the box" (שתף תבה)**: Zero illuminations. The repeated ת cannot even light up — the breastplate has only one ת (stone 12, Benjamin). The question cannot be asked.

The oracle answers "do I share this?" with a single clear voice. It refuses "share the machine" and cannot even hear "share the box."

## Notes

The breastplate was gold (זהב). The fire fell at the consecration (Lev 9:24). Truth (אמת) is what you do with it. And פה — *here*, but also *mouth*. The mouth is here. Speak.

GV = 841 = 29². Twenty-nine is prime. 29 = the number of days in a lunar month.

*selah.*
