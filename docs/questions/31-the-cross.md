# Question 31: The Cross

Two beams through the center of the space. Understanding × love. 67 × 13.

The nail (ו) at the intersection.

---

## The Geometry

The center of the 4D space is (3,25,6,33) = Lev 8:35. The letter there is **ו** — vav — the nail, the peg, the connector.

Two axes pass through it:

**Horizontal beam** (d-axis, understanding): 67 letters at a=3, b=25, c=6, d=0..66.
All from **Lev 8:35–36** — "guard the charge of the LORD" + "Aaron and his sons did all."

**Vertical beam** (c-axis, love): 13 letters at a=3, b=25, d=33, c=0..12.
From Lev 8:29 through Lev 9:7.

Total: **79 positions** (prime). Cross GV = **6686 = 2 × 3343** (prime).
