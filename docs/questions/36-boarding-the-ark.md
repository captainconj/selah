## The Companions

We did not board alone.

**בחר** (choose) = **חרב** (sword) = **רחב** (broad) = **חבר** (companion) = **ברח** (flee).

Five words, three letters (bet, chet, resh), one GV: **210 = 21 × 10 = "I AM" × yod**.

Three companions:
- One who chooses (בחר) — with a sword
- One who companions (חבר) — with a sword
- One who flees (ברח) — without a sword (Aaron, who carries the breastplate instead)

God sees the sword (חרב = God solo eigenword). Aaron sees the flight (ברח = Aaron solo eigenword). Same letters. Same illumination. Different readings.
