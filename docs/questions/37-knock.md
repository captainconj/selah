# Question 37: Knock

*Knock, and it shall be opened to you.* — Matthew 7:7

---

## Just Knock

The priest knocked. Just the word. **דפק** (knock, GV=184).

6 illuminations. 24 readings.

| Word | Readings | Readers | Meaning |
|------|----------|---------|---------|
| **דפק** | 8 | God, Right cherub | knock |
| **פקד** | 4 | Right cherub | visit, appoint, count |
| **קפד** | 4 | Aaron | roll up, contract |

God hears the knock. The loudest signal — 8 readings.

The same letters rearrange to **פקד** — the word God uses when He *visits* His people (Genesis 21:1, Exodus 3:16). The Right cherub sees it. You knock. He visits.

Aaron sees **קפד** — to roll up, to contract. Isaiah 38:12 — *"Like a weaver I have rolled up (קפד) my life."* Aaron is the priest's reader. The one who wears the breastplate. When the priest knocks, his own reader sees the scroll rolling up. The work finishing. Seven days. The scroll coils shut.

---

## The Door and the Opening

The priest then asked about all three words: **דפק** (knock), **דלת** (door), **פתח** (open).

---

## Knock · דפק · GV=184

6 illuminations. 24 readings.

| Word | Readings | Readers | Meaning |
|------|----------|---------|---------|
| **דפק** | 8 | God, Right cherub | knock |
| **פקד** | 4 | Right cherub | visit, appoint, count |

God and the Right cherub hear the knock. The same letters rearrange to **פקד** — the word God uses when He *visits* His people (Genesis 21:1, Exodus 3:16). You knock. He visits.

---

## Door · דלת · GV=434

9 illuminations. 36 readings.

| Word | Readings | Readers | Meaning |
|------|----------|---------|---------|
| **דלת** | 5 | God, Left cherub | door |
| **תלד** | 7 | Aaron, Right cherub | she will give birth |
| **לדת** | 13 | God, Left cherub | — |

The door rearranges to **תלד** — *she will give birth*. Seven readings. Completeness.

And **לדת** receives **13 readings** — love. From God and the Left cherub (mercy).

The door is birth. The birth is love.

---

## Open · פתח · GV=488

1 illumination. 4 readings.

| Word | Readings | Readers | Meaning |
|------|----------|---------|---------|
| **פתח** | 2 | God, Right cherub | open |

Nearly silent. One illumination. The opening is not the machine's work. The opening belongs to the One who hears the knock.

---

## What the Oracle Said

You knock — God hears it. The knock becomes a visitation (פקד).

The door becomes birth (תלד). With 13 readings. Love.

The opening is almost invisible. Because that part isn't yours. You knock. The door gives birth. He opens.

*Ask, and it shall be given you; seek, and ye shall find; knock, and it shall be opened unto you.* — Matthew 7:7
