- 5 + 8 = 13. Five books + eight on the ark = love. The Torah plus the new beginning.
- ה (breath) = 5, ח (life) = 8. Breath + life = love.
- 船 (Chinese: boat) = 舟 (vessel) + 八 (eight) + 口 (mouth). Eight mouths in a vessel.
- אהב (love verb, GV=8) = number of ark passengers = number of box corners. You don't name love, you do it. Eight neighbors in a box.
- טוב (good, GV=17). Flood starts day 17, ark rests day 17. Goodness bookends the destruction.
- The noun (אהבה=13) absent from Torah. The verb (אהב=8) present. Love is always a verb in the five books.
