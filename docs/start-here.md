# Start Here

*A first path into Selah for a reader who was not here for the discovery.*

Type: `guided reproduction`
State: `clean but needs better presentation`

---

Selah is easiest to misunderstand if you enter it sideways.

This repo contains:

- a canonical Hebrew text model
- a 4D coordinate construction over the Torah
- an oracle built from the breastplate
- basin dynamics over Torah vocabulary
- a code/map layer connecting the breastplate to amino-acid structure
- protein playback through that same instrument
- and a large amount of synthesis, reflection, and testimony built on top of those layers

The right way in is not to read everything.
It is to walk a small honest path.

## What To Expect

This path is designed to help you do three things:

1. understand what kind of documents you are reading
2. reproduce at least a few strong results
3. see the structure before you are asked to contemplate larger interpretations

## The Rule While Reading

Keep asking:

- what is the source text or source sequence?
- what computation was run?
- what saved artifact anchors the claim?
- where does English help enter?
- where does interpretation begin?

If you keep those layers separate, the repo becomes much easier to follow.
