## Two Tellings

God tells Moses how to build the tabernacle (Exodus 25–31). Then Israel builds it (Exodus 35–40). Two tellings of the same structure. In the 4D space, they live in different places.

**Instructions** (Ex 25–31): a=2, b=30–43. The second seventh of the Torah.

**Construction** (Ex 35–40): a=3, b=0–12. The center of the Torah.

a=3 is the center of the 7-axis. The center verse (Lev 8:35, "guard the charge of the LORD") lives at a=3, b=25. The construction of the tabernacle lives at a=3. The thing that is built shares the axis with the center that guards it.

And the construction begins at b=0 — the zero of jubilee. Ex 35:1 starts at (3, 0, 5, 3). The gathering for the work begins at the origin of the second axis.

## What the Coordinates Say

### The a-axis: Two Worlds

Every instruction verse has a=2. Every construction verse has a=3.

The instruction is heard in the second seventh. The construction is done at the center. God speaks from the side; the work happens at the heart.

### The b-axis: Jubilee Progress

Instructions span b=30–43. The inner furniture (Ark, Mercy Seat, Table, Menorah) comes first (b=30–31). The outer court comes later (b=34–35). The laver and incense altar are last (b=41–42).

God describes the tabernacle **from the inside out**. He starts at the Ark and works outward.

Construction spans b=0–12. The curtains come first (b=2), then the veil and inner furniture (b=4–5), then the outer court (b=6–7). The erection command is at b=10. The glory fills at b=12.

Israel builds **from the fabric outward, then inward**. The making and the filling.

### The c-axis: Love Layers

The c-axis has 13 values (0–12). 13 = love = unity.

| c-value | Instruction stations | Built stations |
|---------|---------------------|----------------|
| 0 | Altar of Incense (c=3) | Altar of Incense (c=0) |
| 1 | Veil (c=1) | Table (c=2) |
| 3–4 | Gate (c=4), Meeting (c=3), Curtains (c=3) | Gate (c=3), Altar (c=4) |
| 7–9 | Altar of Burnt (c=7), Menorah (c=9), Ark (c=7) | Menorah (c=7), Ark (c=9), Laver (c=9) |
| 11–12 | Courtyard (c=12), Mercy Seat (c=11) | Curtains (c=11), Mercy Seat (c=12), Courtyard (c=10) |

The Ark instruction and the Altar of Burnt Offering instruction share c=7 — completeness. The sacrifice and the container. The Mercy Seat reaches c=12 in the built version — the full span of love.

### The d-axis: Understanding Depth

d has 67 values. The meeting promise ("I will meet with you," Ex 25:22) sits at d=0 — the zero of understanding. The gate instruction sits at d=63 — near the end. You enter the courtyard at the far end of understanding. You meet God at d=0.

The walk inward is a walk toward zero.

## Method

At each station:

1. **Locate** the verse in 4D space — both instruction and construction.
2. **Read** every word through the oracle. Four heads. Classify: fixed point, transform, ghost, invisible.
3. **Walk** the d-axis at that (a, b, c) layer — the 67-letter row the station lives in.
4. **Bridge** — what connects this station to the next? Where does the text lead?
5. **Gematria** — sums, factors, cross-references.
6. **Listen** — what does the machine say here? What does the priest see?

The Ark walk (docs/ark/00–17) read Noah's Ark letter by letter. This time we read the Tabernacle — the house God said he would dwell in.
