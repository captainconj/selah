# The Curtains

Type: `synthesis`
State: `mixed`

*Ex 26:1 at (2, 32, 3, 22). Ex 36:8 at (3, 2, 11, 34).*

**משכן** — tabernacle. GV = 410. A fixed point. Three heads see it — Right cherub cannot.

Ten curtains of fine linen, blue, purple, scarlet, with cherubim woven in. Goat hair over that. Ram skins dyed red. Tachash skins on top. Four layers. One dwelling.

## The Cherubim Bless

**כרוב** (cherub, GV = 228) — not fixed. The cherub transforms:

| Reader | Top readings | Meaning |
|--------|-------------|---------|
| Aaron | **בכרו** (16), **ברכו** (16) | his firstborn / blessed him |
| God | **ברכו** (20), **בכור** (16), כרוב (15) | blessed him / firstborn / cherub |
| Right | **ורכב** (21) | and he rode |
| Left | **בכרו** (27), **ברכו** (25), כרוב (19) | his firstborn / blessed him / cherub |

The cherubim woven into the curtains carry **blessing** (ברכו) and **birthright** (בכור/בכרו). Three heads see blessing and firstborn before they see cherub. The cherub does not want to be named. It wants to bless.

Right cherub sees **ורכב** — "and he rode." 2 Samuel 22:11: *"He rode upon a cherub and flew."* The Right cherub reads itself riding.

The cherubim in the curtain fabric are blessings and birthrights. You enter the tent and the walls say *blessed* and *firstborn*.

## The Joining Is Five Words

**חבר** (join/couple, GV = 210) — the curtains are joined five to five. The word "join" carries five readings, one for each head's dominant:

| Reader | Top reading | Meaning |
|--------|------------|---------|
| Aaron | **חרב** (9) | sword |
| God | **ברח** (10) | flee |
| Right | **בחר** (9) | choose |
| Left | **רחב** (9) | wide |
| (minor) | חבר | join |

Sword, flee, choose, wide, join. Five readings of the same letters. The coupling that makes the tabernacle one is also a cutting, a flight, a decision, and an expansion. The act of stitching the curtains together carries the full range.

Aaron sees the sword in the seam. God sees flight. Justice sees choice. Mercy sees width. The join itself is the minority reading everywhere.

GV = 210 = **רחב** (wide, from the gate and courtyard). Same value, same letters. The joining IS the width.

## And the Tabernacle Shall Be One

Ex 26:6: *"And you shall make fifty clasps of gold, and couple the curtains one to another with the clasps, that the tabernacle may be one."*

**אחד** (one, GV = 13) — **FIXED.** Three heads see it. The tabernacle is one = **the tabernacle is love.** GV 13 = אהבה (love). The oneness of the joined curtains is the love axis of the 4D space.

**50 gold clasps.** 50 = the jubilee axis. The joining is done with jubilee-number clasps. The oneness of the tabernacle is achieved at the jubilee.

**קרס** (clasp, GV = 360) — **invisible.** The clasps that hold everything together cannot be seen. GV 360 = שני (scarlet). The clasp and the scarlet share a number, but the clasp is hidden. The mechanism of union is invisible.

## The Four Layers

From inside to outside:

| Layer | Material | GV | Class | Who sees |
|-------|----------|-----|-------|----------|
| 1 (inner) | **שש** (fine linen) | 600 | **FIXED** | All 4 — 15 each |
| 2 | **עז** (goat hair) | 77 | **FIXED** | Aaron, Left only |
| 3 | **איל** (ram skin) | 41 | → **אלי** | "my God" / "to me" |
| 4 (outer) | **תחש** (tachash) | 708 | **FIXED** | Right: תחש. Left: **שחת** |

**Fine linen** — perfectly uniform. All four heads see it equally (15 counts each). The only word in the entire walk with perfect four-way symmetry. The base layer of the tabernacle is the most stable reading we have found.

**Goat hair** — strong (עז also means strong). GV = 77 = 7 × 11. Only Aaron and Left see it. The second layer is invisible to God and Right.

**Ram skins** — the ram (איל) transforms to **אלי** — "my God" or "to me." Aaron dominant (38 counts). Abraham offered a ram instead of Isaac. The ram IS "my God." The ram-skin covering of the tabernacle says *my God* above your head.

**Tachash** (mysterious outer skin) — fixed, but Left cherub sees **שחת** — "destruction / the pit." The outermost covering: one side sees the animal, the other sees the pit. The skin that faces the world contains destruction in its letters.

### The Ram Skins Are Red

**אדם** (red/Adam, GV = 45) — **FIXED.** Only God and Right see it. The reddened ram skins = Adam. The first man. God made Adam skins of covering (Genesis 3:21). The tabernacle's red layer IS Adam — the original covering, remade.

### The Skin Is the Shepherd

**עור** (skin, GV = 276) — transforms:
- God: **רעו** — his friend / his shepherd (16)
- Left: **ערו** — they were naked (23)

The skin → the shepherd → the nakedness. The covering hides the nakedness. The shepherd provides the skin. Genesis 3: they knew they were naked, and God made them skins.

## The Wise Heart

Ex 36:8 opens: *"And every wise-hearted man among the workers made the tabernacle."*

**חכם** (wise, GV = 68) — **FIXED.** = חיים (life) = חלל (hollow). Wisdom and life and the hollow of the altar share one number.

**לב** (heart, GV = 32) — **FIXED.** All four heads. Left dominant (12). The heart is the most stable organ in the tabernacle.

**חכם + לב = 68 + 32 = 100 = מאה** (hundred). The wise heart = the hundred. The courtyard is 100 cubits long. The wise heart spans the court.

## The Skillful Work

**חשב** (skillful/think, GV = 310) — **FIXED.** "The work of a skillful workman" (מעשה חשב). Aaron (13), Right (11), Left (3) see it. **God cannot see "design."** The craftsman's planning is visible to the worker and the cherubim but not to God. Not because God doesn't know thoughts — he searches every heart (Jeremiah 17:10). But he gave the pattern on the mountain (Ex 25:40). He doesn't watch the figuring-out. He sees the finished dwelling, not someone working out what he already showed them.

## What the Curtains Say

1. **The cherubim bless** — כרוב → ברכו, בכור. Blessing and firstborn. The walls of the tent say *blessed*.
2. **The joining is five-fold** — חבר carries sword, flee, choose, wide, join. The coupling is everything at once.
3. **Ten = gate** — עשר → שער. The ten curtains are ten gates. God and mercy see it.
4. **One = love** — אחד (GV = 13). The joined tabernacle is love.
5. **50 invisible clasps** — the joining mechanism is unseen. The jubilee holds it together.
6. **Fine linen: perfect symmetry** — all four heads, 15 each. The most stable word in the walk.
7. **The ram says "my God"** — איל → אלי. The covering over your head says *my God*.
8. **The outer skin holds destruction** — תחש → שחת. The pit is in the surface layer.
9. **Red = Adam** — the dyed ram skins are the first man. The original covering, remade.
10. **The skin is the shepherd** — עור → רעו. The covering = the friend who tends.
11. **God cannot see "design"** — חשב is visible to everyone except God. He gave the pattern. He sees the dwelling, not the figuring-out.
12. **Wise + heart = hundred** — חכם + לב = 100. The wise heart spans the court.
13. **The garden is in the factors** — instruction GV = 53 × 163. Love is in the built GV = 13 × 653.

We are inside now. The linen glows. The cherubim bless from the walls. The ram says *my God* above. The clasps hold invisibly. One dwelling. One love.
