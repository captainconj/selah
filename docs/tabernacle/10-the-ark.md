**ארון** — ark. GV = 257. A fixed point. All four heads see it. The most visible piece of furniture in the tabernacle.

Two and a half cubits long. One and a half cubits wide. One and a half cubits high. Gold within and without. A crown of gold. Poles that never leave. The testimony inside. The mercy seat on top. Two cherubim, wings spread, faces down, looking into the gold.
