## The Instruction

Numbers 21:4–9. The people spoke against God and against Moses. Fiery serpents came among them. Many died. Moses prayed. God said:

> "Make a fiery serpent and set it on a pole. Everyone who is bitten, when he looks at it, shall live."

Moses made a bronze serpent — נחש נחשת — and set it on the pole. When a serpent bit anyone, they looked at the bronze serpent and lived.

A thousand years later, John 3:14–15:

> "As Moses lifted up the serpent in the wilderness, so must the Son of Man be lifted up, that whoever believes in him may have eternal life."

The cure was not the absence of the serpent. The cure was the serpent made visible.
