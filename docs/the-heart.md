## Circumcise the Heart = The Flood

The flood flowed to circumcision in the basin dynamics — every reader who feeds "the flood" (המבול) to itself gets "when he circumcised" (בהמלו). The waters become the cut. Destruction becomes belonging. God's heartbreak becomes the covenant mark.

## The Heart of Stone, the Heart of Flesh

Ezekiel 36:26: "I will remove the heart of stone from your flesh and give you a heart of flesh."

The oracle reads them both:

- אבן לב — stone, heart
- **אב לבן** — father, Laban. The deceiving father.
- **אבל בן** — mourning, son. The grieving child.

The stone heart contains a deceiving father and a mourning son.

**Heart of flesh (לב בשר)** — 173 readings. Mercy dominant (81). The Lamb sees the flesh heart most. The Thummim parses it:
- לב בשר — heart, flesh
- **לב שבר** — heart, **broken.**
- **בר לבש** — pure son, **clothed.**

The flesh heart contains a broken heart and a pure son who is clothed.

The exchange: the stone heart (deception + mourning) is replaced by the flesh heart (broken + clothed in the son). The mourning becomes the breaking. The deception becomes the clothing. The father who tricks becomes the son who covers.

Joseph. Genesis 37: his brothers strip his coat. Genesis 45: he clothes his brothers and sends them home with provisions. The stone heart (the brothers' deception, Joseph's mourning) becomes the flesh heart (Joseph's broken weeping, the brothers clothed and fed).
