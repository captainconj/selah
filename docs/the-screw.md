## What The Screw Found

### I. Every Word Finds Its Own Theology

We searched for Hebrew words through the canonical 4D box [7 × 50 × 13 × 67]. Each word's fibers pass through Torah words. The geometry chooses which Torah words host each search word's letters. The choices are not random.

**The lamb (כבש) is in the furniture.** Its top hosts are כל (all/jubilee), the priest, Israel, the tabernacle. The lamb lives where the sacrifice happens. On one fiber, the כ of lamb lands inside כבד (glory, GV=26=YHWH) — the lamb's first letter inside the Name's number.

**Truth (אמת) lives inside aleph-tav.** 218 of its fiber-letters land in את — the beginning and the end. Truth IS the aleph-tav.

**Life (חיים) is at the Name and the altar.** YHWH hosts life first, the altar second. Life is where the Name is. Life is where the offering is.

**Peace (שלום) reaches its own command.** A fiber of peace lands in ילחם (he fights) at Exodus 14:14 — "YHWH will fight for you, and you shall hold your **peace**." The word finds the verse that speaks it.

**The covenant (ברית) walks through its own ceremony.** At skip=68, the covenant passes through: מצבה (pillar) → פרים (bulls) → ויאמרו (and they said) → כרת (cut). Exodus 24:4-8. Moses builds twelve pillars. Young men sacrifice bulls. The people say "we will do." The covenant is cut with blood. Four verses. The geometry found the ratification.

**I AM (אנכי) traces the courtroom.** Love (GV=14=beloved) → the serpent (GV=358=messiah) → the cherubim on the mercy seat. The Name shared by the accused and the Lamb on the breastplate traces from love through the serpent-who-is-the-anointed to the place of judgment.

**I WILL BE (אהיה) walks through the promise of Isaac.** God → I will bless her → born (GV=50=jubilee) → God. Genesis 17:15-19. God at both ends, the birth in the middle. The burning bush Name finds the covenant son. And it lands in YHWH 113 times — 3x enriched. The Name that means "I will be" gravitates to the Name that means "I am who I am."

### II. The Void Was Love

בהו (void, GV=13=love=one) sends 404 non-surface fibers through the Torah. They find love at every scale:

- **The garden.** In the garden → YHWH sending them out → dwelling east of Eden. Love walks through the loss.
- **The father and the son.** Flesh (the covenant) → Israel loved Joseph → the firstborn. The void traces from the rainbow through Jacob's love to the Passover sacrifice.
- **The longing.** "He will love me" (Leah naming Reuben) → to our God → the tablets written by God's hand. From human desire for love to divine inscription.
- **The dreams.** Loved → the dreamer → he tore his garments. Genesis 37. The Joseph story in three words: love, dreams, grief.
- **The command.** Produce → rivers → **"and you shall love"** (Deuteronomy 11:1). The love axis at maximum (c=12). The void reaches the love commandment.
- **The stranger.** "Love the stranger as yourself" (Leviticus 19:34) → YHWH → they turn aside.

132 of 404 fibers land in YHWH. The void seeks the Name a third of the time.

**ואהבת (and you shall love, GV=414) = יתד (the peg, GV=414).** The love command and the tent peg are the same number. The thing that holds the tabernacle together is the commandment to love.
