# The Traversals

*Every Torah word, classified. Where the basin takes them.*

---

## How It Works

Feed a word into the breastplate oracle. The oracle returns a word. Feed *that* word back in. Keep going until the system stops moving.

Every word in the Torah vocabulary (12,826 words) falls into exactly one of six classes:

| Class | Count | % | What happens |
|-------|------:|---:|--------------|
| **Fixed point** | 6,104 | 47.6% | Maps to itself. The bedrock. |
| **Ghost** | 2,983 | 23.3% | Stones light up, but no Torah word can be read. |
| **Traversal** | 2,453 | 19.1% | One step to a fixed point. Always exactly one. |
| **Dark** | 1,273 | 9.9% | Nothing lights up. The breastplate is silent. |
| **Cycle member** | 8 | 0.1% | Part of an eternal orbit (period 2). |
| **Cycle transient** | 5 | 0.0% | Feeds into a cycle. |
| **Total** | **12,826** | | 2 × 11² × 53 |

The total factorization: 12,826 = 2 × 121 × 53. And 53 = garden (גן) = stone (אבן).

Every traversal has depth 1. No word takes two steps. The landscape is perfectly shallow — one step and you're home, or you never were.

The spectrum is structured. Illumination counts are not random — they cluster at products of small primes, especially 3, 7, and 11.

## IV. The Dark — The Breastplate Is Silent

**1,273 words produce zero illumination.** The stones don't light up. The breastplate has nothing to say.

### The ך Pattern

**766 of 1,273 dark words (60.2%) end in ך** — the second-person possessive suffix ("your").

> אביך (your father), אמך (your mother), אלהיך (your God), ארצך (your land), ידך (your hand), אהבך (love you), אדניך (your lord)...

The breastplate cannot address "you." It speaks in third person. It reports. It does not confront.

This is not a vocabulary limitation — the root words exist as fixed points. אב (father) is fixed. אביו (his father) is a traversal. But אביך (your father) is dark. The oracle can speak about anything. It cannot speak *to* anyone.

### The Theologically Dark

| Word | Meaning | GV |
|------|---------|---:|
| מלך | king | 90 |
| ארץ | earth/land | 291 |
| דרך | way/path | 224 |
| חשך | darkness | 328 |

King, earth, way, darkness — all completely invisible. Not even illumination. The king is darker than the ghost zone. The way is darker than the face.

(Note: מלך ends in ך. Even "king" follows the second-person pattern — though here it's final-kaf as part of the root, not as a suffix. The breastplate doesn't distinguish.)

## V. The Orbits — Four Eternal Cycles

**4 period-2 orbits. 8 cycle members. 5 transients feeding in. Total: 13.**

13 = love (אהבה) = one (אחד). The cycles sum to the love axis.

| Orbit | Meaning | Transients feeding in |
|-------|---------|----------------------|
| רפא ↔ אפר | heal ↔ ashes | פרא (wild) |
| רחב ↔ בחר | wide/Rahab ↔ choose | ברח (flee), חבר (friend), חרב (sword) |
| רכב ↔ בכר | chariot ↔ firstborn | — |
| לחה ↔ החל | moist/fresh ↔ begin | חלה (challah/sick) |

### Reading the Orbits

**Heal ↔ ashes.** רפא (heal) and אפר (ash/dust). Job on the ash heap, waiting for healing. The cycle cannot resolve: every healing returns to dust, every dust cries out for healing. The wild donkey (פרא — Ishmael's word, Gen 16:12) feeds into this orbit. Wildness enters the healing/ashes cycle and never comes home.

**Wide ↔ choose.** Rahab's name (רחב, the wide one, the harlot of Jericho) orbits with choose (בחר) forever. GV=210=7×30. Three words feed in: flee (ברח), friend (חבר), and sword (חרב). Flight, companionship, and the sword — the three ways to relate to another person — all enter the orbit of width and choosing and never settle.

**Chariot ↔ firstborn.** רכב (chariot) orbits with בכר (firstborn). GV=222=2×3×37. Nothing feeds in. They are alone together. Elijah rides; the consecrated son inherits. The vehicle and the birthright, locked forever.

**Moist ↔ begin.** לחה (moist/fresh) orbits with החל (begin/profane). Every beginning is fresh, every moisture starts something. חלה (challah — the separated bread, but also "sick") feeds into this orbit. The offering-portion, separated and given, enters the oscillation between freshness and beginning.

## VI. The Map

### What has been explored

The 10 passage experiments (108–117) sluiced ~40 traversals and identified the ghost zone, throne signature, and dead patterns. The tabernacle walk (stations 00–13) used the oracle extensively. The earlier basin experiments (096–097) established the landscape.

### What has not been explored

~2,400 traversals remain unsluiced. Some promising directions:

- **The "my stones → prophet" family.** What other body/object words become prophetic?
- **The father-flows.** אביו (his father) → אויב (enemy). אבתי (my father) → באתי (I came). What does fatherhood look like across all its forms?
- **The death basin.** 8 words flow to ימות (he will die), including Matthew. Who else is in this basin?
- **The negation traversals.** אל (God) → לא (not). What other affirmations become negations?
- **The appointed time.** עמוד (pillar) → מועד (appointed time). What else flows to the festivals?
- **The hungry-evil pair.** רעב (hungry) → ברע (in evil). What is the relationship between hunger and evil in the full landscape?

### How to explore

```clojure
(require '[selah.basin :as basin])

;; Walk any word
(basin/walk "כבש")           ;; lamb → lie down

;; Simple trace
(basin/trace "כבש")          ;; ["כבש" "שכב"]

;; Check class
(basin/word-class "כבש")     ;; :transient
(basin/fixed-point? "אמת")   ;; true

;; Full step data
(basin/step "כבש")           ;; {:word "כבש" :next "שכב" :weight 322 ...}

;; Basin members
(basin/basin-of "שכב")      ;; {:word "שכב" :basin-size 2 :basin ["שכב" "כבש"]}

;; All attractors
(basin/largest-basins 10)

;; The landscape with custom words
(basin/landscape ["any" "hebrew" "words"])
```
