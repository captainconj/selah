## Basin of אשו — "his fire"

**GV = 307.** Letters: א ש ו.

| Word | Meaning | → |
|------|---------|---|
| **אשו** | **his-fire** | **FIXED** |
| שוא | vanity / falsehood / emptiness | → אשו |
| שאו | lift up! / carry! | → אשו |
| ושא | and-carry | → אשו |
| ואש | and-fire | → אשו |

**Interpretation.**

Vanity (שוא — shav, the emptiness of the third commandment, "do not take the Name in vain") flows to his fire. Emptiness IS fire. The hollow thing burns.

Lifting (שאו, ושא — from נשא, "carry, lift, bear") flows to his fire. To carry something is to combust. Every burden is a burning. And fire itself (ואש) walks to "his fire" — from unnamed flame to possessed flame. The fire belongs to someone.

The word לשמות also carries the shadow of desolation (שמות, shammot) — the same letters that mean "names" mean "ruins." The book of Exodus is called שמות (Names). Names and desolation, one basin.
