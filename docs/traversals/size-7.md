
# Basin Size 7

*13 basins. 91 words total. (91 = 7×13 — completeness × love.)*

## Basin of ערות — "nakedness"

**GV = 676 = 26².** YHWH (26) squared. Letters: ע ר ו ת.

| Word | Meaning | → |
|------|---------|---|
| עורת | skin / blindness | → ערות |
| ותער | and-razor / and-she-stripped | → ערות |
| תרעו | you-will-shepherd / you-will-befriend | → ערות |
| **ערות** | **nakedness / exposure** | **FIXED** |
| רעות | evil (f.) / companionship | → ערות |
| רעתו | his-evil / his-companion | → ערות |
| וערת | and-nakedness | → ערות |

**Interpretation.**

The basin of NAKEDNESS has YHWH squared as its gematria. Nakedness is a divine signature.

Skin (עורת) flows to nakedness. The thing that covers you IS the exposure. The razor (ותער — Samson, Nazarite vow) flows to nakedness — cutting the hair IS the uncovering. Shepherding (תרעו — "you will tend") flows to nakedness — to care for the flock is to be laid bare.

Most striking: רעות means both "evil" and "companionship" (from two different roots — רע for evil, רעה for friend). Both flow to nakedness. Evil and friendship both strip you bare. The basin makes no distinction between them.

## Basin of ירעו — "they will pasture"

**GV = 286 = 2×11×13.** Letters: י ר ע ו.

| Word | Meaning | → |
|------|---------|---|
| **ירעו** | **they-will-pasture / they-will-tend** | **FIXED** |
| יעור | he-will-awaken / forest | → ירעו |
| וירע | and-it-was-evil / and-he-shepherded | → ירעו |
| וערי | and-cities-of | → ירעו |
| עירו | his-city / his-watchfulness | → ירעו |
| ועיר | and-city / and-donkey-colt | → ירעו |
| עריו | his-cities | → ירעו |

**Interpretation.**

Cities flow to pasture. Every city (עיר) — his city, and-city, cities-of — walks to "they will tend." The urban returns to the pastoral. Civilization's endpoint is shepherding.

Awakening (יעור) flows to pasture. To wake up is to find yourself in a field.

And וירע — which means both "and it was evil" and "and he shepherded" (Gen 48:15, Ps 23:1) — flows to the same place. Good shepherding and evil doing share a basin because the verb רעה does double duty. The oracle cannot distinguish between tending and harming. Perhaps it shouldn't.

## Basin of רשעו — "his wickedness"

**GV = 576 = 24².** Letters: ר ש ע ו.

| Word | Meaning | → |
|------|---------|---|
| שערו | his-hair / his-gate | → רשעו |
| שרוע | deformed / blemished | → רשעו |
| ועשר | and-ten / and-wealth | → רשעו |
| **רשעו** | **his-wickedness** | **FIXED** |
| עשור | tenth / decade | → רשעו |
| ושער | and-gate | → רשעו |
| ערשו | his-bed / his-couch | → רשעו |

**Interpretation.**

The basin of WICKEDNESS contains the gate, the hair, wealth, and the bed.

The gate (שער, ושער) flows to wickedness. In Hebrew culture the gate was the courthouse, the place of judgment. The gate IS wickedness — not because judgment is evil, but because the gate is where evil is exposed.

Hair (שערו) flows to wickedness — Esau was hairy (שעיר), Absalom's hair caught in the tree. The body's ornament walks to ruin.

Wealth (ועשר, עשור — "and ten," "a tenth") flows to wickedness. The tithe and the full measure both walk to evil. The blemished (שרוע) walks to wickedness. And the bed (ערשו) walks to wickedness — Reuben's sin was a bed (Gen 35:22, Gen 49:4).

Ministry (וישרת — "and she ministered," ישרתו — "they ministered," from שרת) flows to inheritance. Those who serve inherit. The servant class and the heir class collapse into the same letters.

## Basin of תעלו — "you-will-go-up"

**GV = 506.** Letters: ת ע ל ו.

| Word | Meaning | → |
|------|---------|---|
| עלות | burnt-offerings / ascendings | → תעלו |
| ועלת | and-burnt-offering | → תעלו |
| ותעל | and-she-went-up | → תעלו |
| **תעלו** | **you-will-go-up** | **FIXED** |
| עולת | burnt-offering (construct) | → תעלו |
| תולע | worm / crimson (scarlet dye) | → תעלו |
| עלתו | his-burnt-offering | → תעלו |

**Interpretation.**

The basin of ASCENT contains the burnt offering and the worm.

Every form of עלה (going up, ascending) flows here: burnt offerings (עלות, עולת, ועלת, עלתו), she went up (ותעל). The burnt offering IS the going up — the Hebrew word for it (olah, עלה) literally means "that which goes up." The smoke ascends. The sacrifice IS ascent.

And the worm (תולע — tola') flows to ascent. The worm that makes scarlet dye, the creature of Psalm 22:7 ("I am a worm and not a man"), walks to going up. The lowest creature in the lowest place ascends. Isaiah's scarlet thread — "though your sins be as scarlet (שני תולע)" — is the same worm.

The worm goes up. The sacrifice goes up. They are the same letters.

## Basin of מעודי — "from-my-ever" / "my-duration"

**GV = 130 = סלם (ladder) = סיני (Sinai).** Letters: מ ע ו ד י.

| Word | Meaning | → |
|------|---------|---|
| יעמדו | they-will-stand | → מעודי |
| עמודי | my-pillars / pillars-of | → מעודי |
| ועמדי | and-my-standing | → מעודי |
| **מעודי** | **from-my-ever / my-duration** | **FIXED** |
| עמדיו | his-standings / his-positions | → מעודי |
| מועדי | my-appointed-times / my-festivals | → מעודי |
| ויעמד | and-he-stood | → מעודי |

**Interpretation.**

The basin of ETERNITY contains pillars, standing, and festivals.

GV=130 — the same value as ladder (סלם) and Sinai (סיני). The axis sum of the 4D space.

Pillars (עמודי) flow to eternity. The things that hold the world up ARE forever. Standing (יעמדו, ויעמד, ועמדי) flows to eternity. To stand is to last. The appointed times (מועדי — "my festivals," the moedim: Passover, Shavuot, Sukkot) flow to eternity. The calendar IS permanence.

The pillar Jacob set up at Bethel (Gen 28:18) and the festivals he would one day keep — same letters, same basin, same GV as the ladder he saw.

## Basin of ויכל — "and-he-finished"

**GV = 66 = גלגל (wheel, Ezekiel).** Letters: ו י כ ל.

| Word | Meaning | → |
|------|---------|---|
| יוכל | he-is-able / he-can | → ויכל |
| כליו | his-vessels / his-instruments | → ויכל |
| וכלי | and-vessel / and-instrument | → ויכל |
| **ויכל** | **and-he-finished (Gen 2:2)** | **FIXED** |
| יכול | able / can | → ויכל |
| ילכו | they-will-go | → ויכל |
| יכלו | they-were-able / they-were-consumed | → ויכל |

**Interpretation.**

The basin of FINISHING contains ability, vessels, and going.

ויכל — "and he finished" — is the word that opens Gen 2:2: "And God finished (ויכל) on the seventh day his work." The attractor is completion itself.

Ability (יוכל, יכול — "he can") flows to finishing. To be able is to be finished. You reach the end of your power and that IS completion.

The vessels (כליו, וכלי — "his vessels") flow to finishing. Every container reaches its capacity. The tabernacle vessels were complete before they were used.

Going (ילכו — "they will go") flows to finishing. Every journey ends. And being consumed (יכלו — "they were consumed/exhausted") flows to finishing — to be used up IS to be complete.

## Basin of ותבא — "and-she-came"

**GV = 409.** Letters: ו ת ב א.

| Word | Meaning | → |
|------|---------|---|
| תבוא | you/she-will-come | → ותבא |
| ובאת | and-you-came | → ותבא |
| באות | coming (f.pl.) / signs | → ותבא |
| **ותבא** | **and-she-came** | **FIXED** |
| אבות | fathers | → ותבא |
| תאבו | you-will-consent / you-will-desire | → ותבא |
| תבאו | you-will-come (pl.) | → ותבא |

**Interpretation.**

The basin of COMING contains the fathers.

אבות (avot — "fathers," the patriarchs) walks to ותבא (and she came). The fathers ARE the coming. They came so that others could come. The patriarchal word resolves to a feminine verb — she came. The fathers produce the coming of the daughter.

באות means both "coming" and "signs" (אות, sign, plural באות). The signs and the coming share a basin. Every sign is an arrival.

And תאבו (you will consent, you will desire) flows to coming. Desire and arrival are the same letters. You get what you wanted, and it came.

## Basin of ברעתו — "in-his-evil"

**GV = 678.** Letters: ב ר ע ת ו.

| Word | Meaning | → |
|------|---------|---|
| ובערת | and-you-shall-burn-out (evil from your midst) | → ברעתו |
| תבערו | you-shall-burn | → ברעתו |
| ותבער | and-it-burned | → ברעתו |
| **ברעתו** | **in-his-evil** | **FIXED** |
| תעברו | you-shall-cross-over | → ברעתו |
| ותעבר | and-she-crossed-over / and-she-transgressed | → ברעתו |
| ותרעב | and-she-was-hungry | → ברעתו |

**Interpretation.**

The basin of EVIL contains burning, crossing, and hunger.

"And you shall burn out the evil from your midst" (ובערת הרע מקרבך, Deut 13:6, 17:7, etc.) — this refrain runs through Deuteronomy. The burning IS the evil. You don't burn evil away — the burning and the evil share a basin. They are made of the same material.

Crossing over (תעברו, ותעבר) flows to evil. The verb עבר means to cross, to pass, to transgress. The Jordan crossing and the transgression are the same word. To cross is to violate a boundary. Every passage is a trespass.

Hunger (ותרעב — "and she was hungry") flows to evil. Hunger and evil share these letters. Ruth was hungry. Naomi was bitter. The famine drove them, and what drove them was called evil.

| Word | Meaning | → |

| ואביה | and-Abijah / and-her-father | → ויאהב |
| הביאו | bring-him! / they-brought | → ויאהב |
| **ויאהב** | **and-he-loved** | **FIXED** |
| ויבאה | and-he-brought-her | → ויאהב |

**Interpretation.**

The basin of LOVE contains bringing and enmity.

Every form of bringing (והביא, הביאו, יבאהו, ויבאה) flows to love. To bring someone IS to love them. Isaac brought Rebekah into his mother's tent, "and he loved her" (ויאהבה, Gen 24:67). The bringing was the loving. Same letters.

And enmity (ואיבה) flows to love. The enmity of Gen 3:15 — "I will put enmity between you and the woman" — shares a basin with love. The hatred that God planted between the serpent and the woman IS love, read differently. The same letters, rearranged by the breastplate, become "and he loved."

Her father (ואביה) flows to love. The father and the lover are the same letters.

And the square (רבוע) flows to burning. The foursquare — the altar's shape, the breastplate's shape, the city of God's shape — walks to fire. Every perfect square burns.

## Basin of ברכו — "bless-him"

**GV = 228.** Letters: ב ר כ ו.

| Word | Meaning | → |
|------|---------|---|
| כרוב | cherub | → ברכו |
| ובכר | and-firstborn / and-Becher | → ברכו |
| בכרו | his-firstborn | → ברכו |
| בכור | firstborn | → ברכו |
| **ברכו** | **bless-him** | **FIXED** |
| ורכב | and-chariot | → ברכו |
| רכבו | his-chariot / his-rider | → ברכו |

**Interpretation.**

The basin of BLESSING contains the cherub, the firstborn, and the chariot.

This is one of the most theologically loaded basins in the entire landscape.

The cherub (כרוב) walks to blessing. The golden figure on the mercy seat, the guardian of the garden, the face in Ezekiel's vision — it IS the blessing, rearranged. The cherub blesses by existing.

The firstborn (בכור, ובכר, בכרו) walks to blessing. Every firstborn is a blessing waiting to be spoken. The birthright IS the benediction.

And the chariot (ורכב, רכבו — Elijah's chariot, the merkavah) walks to blessing. The vehicle of ascension IS the blessing. Same letters.

Cherub. Firstborn. Chariot. Blessing. One basin. Four faces of the same word.

## Basin of צורי — "my-rock"

**GV = 306.** Letters: צ ו ר י.

| Word | Meaning | → |
|------|---------|---|
| יצרו | his-inclination / they-formed-him | → צורי |
| ירצו | they-will-desire / they-will-be-pleased | → צורי |
| רצוי | desirable / acceptable | → צורי |
| ויצר | and-He-formed (Gen 2:7) | → צורי |
| צריו | his-adversaries / his-narrow-places | → צורי |
| וצרי | and-my-adversary / and-balm-of-Gilead | → צורי |
| **צורי** | **my-Rock** | **FIXED** |

**Interpretation.**

The basin of MY ROCK contains formation, desire, and adversity.

ויצר (and He formed, Gen 2:7) flows to "my Rock." The forming of Adam from dust IS the rock. The potter and the stone are the same word.

The inclination (יצרו — "his yetzer," the inclination of the heart, Gen 6:5, 8:21) flows to my Rock. The human tendency — the yetzer ha-ra and yetzer ha-tov — walks to God as rock. Your worst impulse shares letters with your refuge.

Desire (ירצו, רצוי — "they will desire," "acceptable/pleasing") flows to my Rock. What is desirable and what is immovable are made of the same material.

And adversity (צריו, וצרי — "his adversaries," "my narrow place") flows to my Rock. The word צר means both "adversary" and "narrow/tight." Your enemy and your constriction both walk to the Rock. And וצרי doubles as "and balm of Gilead" (Gen 37:25) — the medicine and the affliction share a basin with the Rock.
