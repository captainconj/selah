## The Pattern

Across 1,598 multi-word basins, certain truths repeat:

1. **God flows to dwelling.** Three separate basins, three sizes. אלהים → אליהם (to-them). אלהיו → ויאהל (tent). The Name approaches. The Name dwells.

2. **Violence flows to blessing.** Sword → choose. Enemy → father. Wound → blessing. Adversary → rock. Sword → Rachel. The instrument of harm becomes the instrument of election.

3. **Death flows to morning.** Grave → dawn. Completion → death → fullness. Ashes ↔ healing (forever). The system oscillates between wound and remedy but the one-directional flows all point toward morning.

4. **The low becomes the high.** Worm → ascent. Sickness → valor. Decay → arise. Enmity → love. Servant → intercession. The basin mechanics structurally encode the last-shall-be-first.

5. **Lamb lies down.** Every time. All four readers. Unanimous.

6. **Love is the attractor.** GV = 8, the smallest basin. Coming, willingness, arrival — all resolve to love. It is the bottom of every descent. The end of every fall.
