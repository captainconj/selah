# The Urim and Thummim — אוּרִים וְתֻמִּים

## The Words

### אורים (Urim) — "Lights"
- Root: אור (light) — same word as Genesis 1:3 "Let there be light"
- Gematria: א(1) + ו(6) + ר(200) + י(10) + ם(40) = **257**

### תמים (Thummim) — "Perfections" / "Completions" / "Truths"
- Root: תמם (to be complete, whole, perfect, blameless)
- Related to תמים (tamim) — "perfect, without blemish" (describes Noah: נח איש צדיק תמים)
- Gematria: ת(400) + מ(40) + י(10) + ם(40) = **490**

### Combined: 257 + 490 = **747**
- With conjunction vav: 257 + 6 + 490 = **753**
- Difference: 490 - 257 = **233** (a Fibonacci number)
- Root אור (light) = 207
- Root תם (wholeness) = 440

Together: **"Lights and Perfections"** — or **"Illumination and Truth"**

Septuagint: δήλωσις καὶ ἀλήθεια — "manifestation and truth"

## Every Biblical Reference

### 1. Exodus 28:30
"And you shall put into the breastplate of judgment the Urim and the Thummim, and they shall be **over Aaron's heart** when he comes before the LORD; and Aaron shall carry the judgment of the sons of Israel **over his heart** before the LORD continually."

Key: They are placed INTO (נתת אל) the breastplate — separate objects inside it. And they rest **over the heart** (על לב אהרן).

### 2. Leviticus 8:8 — THE CENTER OF THE TORAH
"And he placed the breastplate on him, and he put into the breastplate the Urim and the Thummim."

This is the actual investiture of Aaron. At the geometric center of the Torah text. The divine communication device is installed at the center — over the heart.

### 3. Numbers 27:21
"And he [Joshua] shall stand before Eleazar the priest, who shall inquire for him by the judgment of the Urim before the LORD."

Only "Urim" mentioned, not Thummim. Leadership transfer from Moses to Joshua — Joshua gets the Urim rather than direct speech from God. A step down.

### 4. Deuteronomy 33:8
"And of Levi he said: **Your Thummim and Your Urim** belong to Your pious one..."

Order REVERSED here — Thummim before Urim. Moses' blessing on Levi. Possessive: "Your" — they belong to God, entrusted to the Levites.

### 5. 1 Samuel 28:6
"And when Saul inquired of the LORD, the LORD did not answer him, either by dreams, or by Urim, or by prophets."

Three modes of divine communication. Saul is denied all three.

### 6. 1 Samuel 14:41 (Septuagint — critical variant)
"If this guilt is in me or in Jonathan my son, O LORD, give **Urim**. But if this guilt is in your people Israel, give **Thummim**."

Binary oracle: Urim = one answer, Thummim = another. The most explicit description of how they functioned.

### 7. Ezra 2:63 / Nehemiah 7:65
"They should not eat of the most holy things **until a priest stood up with Urim and Thummim**."

By the Second Temple period, the Urim and Thummim were **already lost**. The people are waiting.

## How They Worked — Traditions

### Talmudic (Yoma 73a-73b) — Illuminated Letters
- The 12 breastplate stones bore the names of the 12 tribes
- PLUS: Abraham, Isaac, Jacob (אברהם יצחק ויעקב) and שבטי ישרון
- This ensured **all 22 Hebrew letters** were present on the stones
- When questioned, **individual letters would light up** on the stones
- The priest needed **divine inspiration (ruach hakodesh)** to arrange the lit letters into words
- The Urim "illuminated" (אור) the letters; the Thummim "completed" (תמם) the message

### The Eli/Hannah Story (Yoma 73b) — The Machine Specification
The letters ש,כ,ר,ה lit up. Eli read them as **שכרה** ("she is drunk"). The correct reading was **כשרה** ("like Sarah" — a barren woman praying for a child). Same letters, different arrangement. The oracle requires wisdom to read.

This is not just a story about a misreading. It is a **description of the mechanism**: the oracle is a transposition cipher. No substitution — the letters don't change. Only their arrangement changes. The Urim illuminates which letters matter. The Thummim (completion/truth) is the correct permutation.

The breastplate is a letter grid — 12 stones inscribed with the complete 22-letter alphabet. Letters light up across the grid. The priest reads them in the right order. This is the machine.

### Rashi — Parchment with the Divine Name
A parchment inscribed with the Shem HaMeforash (explicit Name of God) was placed inside the breastplate fold. The Name gave the breastplate its oracular power.

### Academic Consensus — Binary Lot Oracle
Two objects for binary divination — sacred lots. All questions receive yes/no or this/that answers. A 1-bit oracle. The minimal unit of information.

## The Breastplate (חשן המשפט — Breastplate of Judgment)

- Square when folded — a span × a span (~22cm × 22cm)
- **Doubled over** forming a pouch/pocket (where Urim and Thummim placed)
- 4 rows of 3 stones = 12 stones
- Each stone inscribed with a tribal name

| Row | Stone 1 | Stone 2 | Stone 3 |
|-----|---------|---------|---------|
| 1 | אדם (odem/carnelian) | פטדה (pitdah/peridot) | ברקת (bareqet/emerald) |
| 2 | נפך (nofekh/garnet) | ספיר (sappir/lapis) | יהלם (yahalom/crystal) |
| 3 | לשם (leshem/jacinth) | שבו (shevo/agate) | אחלמה (achlamah/amethyst) |
| 4 | תרשיש (tarshish/aquamarine) | שהם (shoham/onyx) | ישפה (yashfeh/jasper) |

1. Reuben, 2. Simeon, 3. Levi, 4. Judah, 5. Dan, 6. Naphtali,
7. Gad, 8. Asher, 9. Issachar, 10. Zebulun, 11. Joseph, 12. Benjamin

### Complete Alphabet Requirement
Without the patriarchs' names and שבטי ישרון, the letter צ (tsade) would be missing from tribal names alone. The breastplate was designed to contain the **complete 22-letter alphabet** — a complete encoding surface.

### The 72-Letter Grid — The Machine

The inscriptions total exactly **72 letters** = 12 stones × 6 letters each:

| Source | Text | Letters |
|--------|------|---------|
| Patriarchs (head) | אברהם יצחק יעקב | 13 |
| 12 Tribes | ראובן שמעון לוי יהודה דן נפתלי גד אשר יששכר זבולן יוסף בנימין | 50 |
| Closing (end) | שבטי ישרון | 9 |
| **Total** | | **72** |

With patriarchs at the head and שבטי ישרון at the end, distributed 6 letters per stone across the 4×3 grid:

```
Stone 1:  אברהםי    Stone 2:  צחקיעק    Stone 3:  בראובן
Stone 4:  שמעוןל    Stone 5:  וייהוד    Stone 6:  הדןנפת
Stone 7:  ליגדאש    Stone 8:  ריששכר    Stone 9:  זבולןי
Stone 10: וסףבני    Stone 11: מיןשבט    Stone 12: יישרון
```

This is a transposition grid. Read by rows, you get the linear inscription. Read by columns, you get a different letter sequence. Read by diagonals, another. **The same 72 letters produce different messages depending on the reading path** — exactly the Eli/Hannah principle at grid scale.

The breastplate is a 4×18 letter matrix (4 rows of 18 letters). When letters "light up" across this grid, the priest must determine the correct reading path through the illuminated positions. The Urim provides the illumination. The Thummim (completion) provides the correct path.

## When They Were Lost

Five things in the First Temple absent from the Second Temple (Sotah 48b, Yoma 21b):
1. The Ark of the Covenant
2. The Mercy Seat (kapporet)
3. The Cherubim
4. The Fire from Heaven
5. **The Urim and Thummim**

Also absent: The Shekhinah and Ruach HaKodesh.

Lost at the destruction of the First Temple (586 BCE). By Ezra/Nehemiah's time, they are waiting for a priest who has them.

## The Missing Letters = LIGHT (אור)

The 12 tribal names contain 18 of the 22 Hebrew letters.

Four are missing: **ח(8) ט(9) צ(90) ק(100)**

Their sum: 8 + 9 + 90 + 100 = **207 = אור (LIGHT)**

This is the root of URIM (אורים = "lights"). The letters that must be ADDED to complete the alphabet — to make the oracle functional — sum to LIGHT itself.

Who provides them:
- **Isaac** (יצחק = 208) provides 3 of 4: ח, צ, ק = 198
  - The sacrifice on Moriah provides the light
- **שבטי** ("tribes of" = 321) provides the last: ט = 9
  - The word that names what they are completes what they lack
- Total added: 198 + 9 = 207 = אור ✓

The tribes alone are incomplete. They need the patriarchs — specifically Isaac, the sacrifice — to provide the light.
